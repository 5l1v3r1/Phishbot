Welcome To our store!

Welcome to Maverick America. Our company is the exclusive US distributor of some of the best brands in the spearfishing market: Salvimar, Pathos, Bleutec, C4, Reef Runner, In Sea, and Picasso.  You will be able to browse through all of the products each brand has to offer, as well as purchase them directly from our website. All products offered have been hand selected by Maverick America after careful testing and review.  We hope you have a great experience and do not hesitate to contact us with suggestions for improvements.

 
 

Salvimar

Salvimar was born in 1962 by the cooperation, dedication, and enthusiasm of a team of people carrying out their job with passion and proficiency. They proudly represent the best of "Made in Italy" as the worlds largest spear shaft producer. 
Shop now!
 
 

Pathos

Stemming from a marine equipment manufacturer, Pathos was conceived in 1996. Owner, Angelos Michalopoulos, has continually developed his products to the highest standards, now producing the lightest footpockets on the market today.  
Shop now!
 
 

Picasso

Picasso has been exclusively dedicated to spearfishing products since its creation in 1986.  The high quality and durability of their products has helped Picasso become one of the most awarded spearfsishing brands in the world. 
Shop now!
 
 

BleuTec

Since inception in 2001, BleuTec has been leading the industry in the construction of composite spearguns and fins. The Greek company strives to ensure that their equipment meets the needs of all modern freedivers and spearfishermen.  
Shop now!
 
 

C4

C4 is known for its long history of innovation and prides itself in creating products that deliver the absolute highest level of performance. Their special processes ensure some of the highest quality carbon equipment in the market. 
Shop now!
 
 

Reef Runner

Since introducing their signature Soft Tips, Reef Runner has become the household name for Toppers,Skins, and accessories alike.  
Shop now!
 
 

In-Sea

Created by waterman Ryan McInnis, In-Sea Apparel captures the essence of all ocean and water-minded individuals.  Offering active gear and everyday apparel, there is something for everyone. 
Shop now!

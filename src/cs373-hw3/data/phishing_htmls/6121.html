Earthworks Business Outlook 2016

by Cristopher Williams | Mar 20, 2016 | Uncategorized

In this series of articles we will put a spotlight on area businesses who have given back to the community in some way. Typically volunteer work, donations, or some other initiative is furthered along by the philanthropic efforts of these local businesses. This is part 1 of 4 for 2016.

The first area business we would like to honor is the Houston chapter of Forklift Safety Solutions and their forklift train the trainer courses. They have lead the way in proper warehouse safety associated with all types of forklift and boom lift applications. Going hand in hand with their forklift training, they encouraged other area businesses to use safety precautions such as installing LED forklift blue lights on all forklifts. Because of this, our area is one of the lowest in the nation of on-the-job deaths associated with forklifts and other heavy machinery. This is why we think they deserve to be honored in this first quarter.

The Illusionists: Witness the Impossible

by Cristopher Williams | Jan 23, 2015 | Upcoming Events

When: Tue, June 2, 2015, 7:30pm – 10:00pm

Where: 615 Louisiana St, Houston, TX 77002

Details:

Jones Hall — Society for the Performing Arts — Seven of the most incredible illusionists on earth perform mind-bending acts of magic and illusion, including many feats never seen before. Each Illusionist is a master in his/her own field. With an assembled cast of 20 sidekicks, they will perform a powerful mix of their most outrageous and unbelievable acts: grand illusion, levitation, mind-reading, disappearance and, for the first time ever, a full view water torture escape. This group of world-class performers takes its cue from the showmanship of the great illusionists of the past, such as Harry Houdini, and pair it with a new and updated contemporary aesthetic, whose set and costume design lend the genre a theatricality and artistry that has rarely been seen before. Don’t miss seeing The Anti-Conjurer, The Trickster, The Escapologist, The Inventor, The Gentleman, The Enchantress and The Surrealist when they come to Houston for the first time. Tickets at 713.227.4SPA or www.spahouston.org.

Anjelah Johnson in Concert

by Cristopher Williams | Jan 23, 2015 | Upcoming Events

When: Sat, March 14, 7:00pm – 9:30pm

Where: 520 Texas Street, Houston, TX 77002

Details:

Bayou Music Center. More information at 713.230.1600 or www.livenation.com

Anjelah Johnson starred in OUR FAMILY WEDDING alongside Carlos Mencia, Regina King, and Oscar winner Forest Whitaker. She can also be seen as a live action character in the film ALVIN AND THE CHIPMUNKS: THE SQUEAKQUEL alongside Jason Lee and the voices of Justin Long, Anna Faris and Amy Poehler.

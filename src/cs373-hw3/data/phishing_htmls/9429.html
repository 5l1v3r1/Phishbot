I don't have my PINsentry card reader

If you don't have your card reader to hand, you can do one of the following:

  • Use the Mobile PINsentry function from the latest version of the Barclays Mobile Banking app1
  • Contact Telephone Banking on 0845 755 55552
  • Log in with your Online Banking 5-digit passcode and memorable word
  • Order an additional or replacement PINsentry card reader by calling the Online Banking Helpdesk on 0845 600 23233

1. You need to be 16 or over and have a Barclays current account and a UK-registered mobile number. You'll also need a web-enabled iPhone or Android smartphone.

2. To maintain a quality service, we may monitor or record phone calls. Read our call charges and information

3. Lines are open 24 hours every day. To maintain a quality service, we may monitor or record phone calls. Read our call charges and information

I've forgotten my log-in details

If you don't use a PINsentry device to log in you can use the options below to get access to your account.

Passcode Forgotten passcode

You can reset your passcode online and we'll post you a new one. We'll just need to ask you a few questions about you and your card first.

While you're waiting for the post, you can use Telephone Banking for your day-to-day banking (if you're registered). Call us on 0845 755 5555.

Reset passcode

Memorable word Forgotten memorable word

If you've forgotten your memorable word, unfortunately you'll need to reset your log-in details by selecting 'Reset passcode' above or call the Online Banking helpdesk on 0845 600 2323 and we'll send you new details in the post.

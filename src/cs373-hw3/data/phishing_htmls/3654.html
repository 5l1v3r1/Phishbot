Take Control with the Telstra 24x7™ App for iPad

Catching public transport, sitting in a cafe, or chilling out on the couch at home - now you can manage your personal Telstra accounts and services when you choose.

App Store Rating @ 4 Sep, 2012
Customer feedback: "Simple, quick, easy to use!"

With Telstra 24x7 App for iPad you can:

  • Securely and easily access all your active services linked to your My Account
  • Keep tabs on your usage if you're on a mobile plan or select BigPond® broadband plan
  • Give your service a nickname
  • Click through to pay your bill online
  • Locate your nearest store
  • Raise a query or chat live with our 24 hour online support team

If you've got a Telstra Pre-Paid service, you can also:

  • Check your balance
  • Recharge your service
  • Purchase Plus Packs

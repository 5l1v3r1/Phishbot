Welcome to Dr. Pandya Podiatry Clinic

Welcome to the Dr. Pandya’s Podiatry Clinic where we aim to provide a friendly and professional service. At Podiatry Clinic, we focus all of our podiatry services and treatment options around providing the highest level of patient care. Dr. Pandya offers a wide range of podiatry services to adults, children, and sports persons. Podiatry Clinic is led by Dr. Pandya, who has years of experience in the industry.

About Dr. Sapna Pandya

Dr. Pandya received her medical degree from the New York College of Podiatric Medicine, and completed her undergraduate training in pre- medicine and biological sciences at the University of Pittsburgh in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania and her three year residency training in Podiatric Medicine and Surgery in New York and New Jersey.

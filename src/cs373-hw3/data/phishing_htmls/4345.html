Portland State University - Single Sign-On

Need Help?

The PSU Single Sign-On (SSO) system enables single sign-on to multiple websites at PSU. This means that you only have to enter your username and password once for websites that subscribe to PSU SSO.

You will need to use your Odin username and password to login to this site. Former students and employees can access transcripts, tax information, etc. at banweb.pdx.edu without an active Odin computer account.

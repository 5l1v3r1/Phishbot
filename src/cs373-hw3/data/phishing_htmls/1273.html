Your Farmers Market & More in Perrysburg

Lievens Market & Outdoor living is the premiere farmers market in the Toledo area, conveniently located in Perrysburg, Ohio.  Providing fresh produce at great prices is what make Lievens Market a favorite destination for area residents.  In addition to produce, Lievens Market also carries a great selection of plants, outdoor living products, garden whimsy, fountains and statuary, memorials, and landscape products.  Stop by and check out all of the great items at  Lievens Market and Outdoor Living.

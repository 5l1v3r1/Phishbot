Update on Municipal Projects

THE FOLLOWING IS A SHORT UPDATE ON CURRENT AND COMPLETED MUNICIPAL PROJECTS

1.  The excavation for the new FIFA Regulation Soccer/Lacrosse Turf Field is about 85% complete.  The facility is scheduled to be completed by mid-summer.

2.  The new retaining wall behind the Pleasant Hills Fire Station is completed.  The fence and guide rail still need to be installed.  Re-paving of the parking lot and restoration is scheduled for spring completion.

3.  The new Wet Weather Equalization Facility near the Toyota Dealership is scheduled to be advertised for bid this spring.  Bid opening in late May to mid-June.  The sanitary sewer replacement in the Sunrise Drive area is scheduled to be bid in August and awarded in September.

4.  Design is ongoing for the renovation of the HVAC in the Municipal Building.  No contract has been awarded yet.

5.  Design is complete for the 60 year old Tassel Lane sanitary sewer pump station and force main.  The project will be bid later this year.

6.  The Arbor Lane storm sewer line replacement is under contract but not completed yet.

7.  This year's road program is anticipated to be Arbor Lane and the parking lots at the Municipal Building.

8.  Tennis courts at Pleasant Kingdom are open and ready for the season.

MS4 - Municipal Separate Storm Sewer System

What is a MS4?

Municipal Separate Storm Sewer System - Any conveyance that is:

Used for collecting and/or conveying stormwater
Owned by a municipality or any other public body
Not used as a combined sewer
Not part of Publically Owned Treatment Works

Please visit the DEP's MS4 website for more details and information.

Senior Presentation – Friday 16 September 2016

Our senior presentation will be held on Friday 16 September 2016 so get your team together to celebrate the end of another great season.  Tickets are $40 each which includes a buffet dinner.

Our junior presentation will be held on Sunday 11 September 2016.  Come along and join in the fun.

 

There will be Bubble Soccer again, a jumping castle as well as a BBQ.
 
Don't Miss The Premier League

Come and watch our very own Winston Hills Premier League sides taking on the best in the area.

 

They're at Max Ruddock every Saturday with the Reserve Grade kicking off at 1pm and the First's at 3pm.

NewSkin Laser - Northridge Medical Spa and Weight Loss

At NewSkin Laser, our philosophy is to treat each patient individually and every single patient is seen by Dr. Alan Darush. After all, everyone’s skin is different and a medical doctor with experience can design a comprehensive plan to care for your skin. Dr. Alan Darush uses combination therapy such as lasers, peels, and injections for optimum results. We offer multiple Botulinum toxins like Botox, Xeomin and Dysport to treat wrinkles in your forehead, crows feet and Glabellar lines.  NewSkin Laser also offers fillers like Juvederm, Restylane, Belotero and Radiesse for facial lifting and lip augmentations.  NewSkin Laser has a new state of the art location in Simi Valley with the latest laser technology for skin tightening, laser hair removal and fraxel laser for resurfacing.  Dr. Alan Darush uses combination of these modalities to achieve natural look with minimal recovery time without breaking the bank.

We have specials on Botox, Restylane, Radiesse and Voluma. Checkout our Prices and our Monthly Specials.

Medical weight loss with close monitoring, diet programs, and medications are prescribed after initial evaluation with our medical director. 

Dr. Alan Darush has been treating patients and doing research on the skin since 2000 and has performed thousands of cosmetic treatments with many loyal and satisfied patients. Experience the difference yourself.  Checkout our Yelp Reviews!!

Most Popular Features

See All Features »

Card Swipe

Streamline the process of tracking hours and attendance by swiping students in and out of events. Card Swipe is compatible with most magnetic card readers, as well as proximity scanners and barcode scanners, and works in both wired and wireless environments.

Read More

Budget Management System

The Budget Management System automates the annual funding request and allocation process for organizations. It can be customized to meet your specific procedures and provides powerful dashboards and reports to help you analyze your incoming and outgoing funds.

Read More

Forms

Our simple design makes it easy to create any type of form, build in smart logic, manage approvals and workflows, and analyze the results of submissions. Some examples of forms you can create include campus-wide surveys, travel and liability waivers, event registrations, and post-event assessments.

Read More

Events + Calendars

We know that you don’t have time to filter through multiple organizational, departmental, or personal calendars to set your schedule. OrgSync’s centralized calendar system makes it easy to stay up-to-date on organization and campus-wide events. The calendar’s crisp, clean design makes filtering and searching for events a breeze.

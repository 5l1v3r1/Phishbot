Congratulations OHIO graduates!

Many hearty congratulations to our graduate and undergraduate students who joined the distinguished ranks of OHIO Alumni! Graduate and Undergraduate Commencement Ceremonies were held April 29 and 30. 

Our Bobcat Pride is bursting at the seams!

Celebrating global diversity, united community

Ohio University's diverse and inclusive community makes it an ideal Home Away From Home for everyone! OHIO's recent International Week celebrated this community with festivities that culminated in the International Street Fair and parade. Check out the story and photos on Compass .  

Take a look at Ohio University

No matter the season, no matter the weather, Ohio University's beautiful campus has a way of drawing you in and making you feel welcome! To see OHIO is to fall in love at first sight! 

Visit this  gallery of images  to see why so many feel this way, and then schedule a visit to see for yourself. 

Access to top teachers, Distinguished Professors

Ohio University is a place where students can connect to top teachers who are recognized for their excellence! 

One of these supportive professors is Gerardine Botte, a Russ Professor of Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering at the Russ College of Engineering and Technology and director of the Russ College’s Center for Electrochemical Engineering, who was just named OHIO's newest Distinguished Professor.

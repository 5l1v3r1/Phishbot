Connecting Learning & Life

Naviance by Hobsons is a comprehensive K-12 college and career readiness platform that enables self-discovery, career exploration, academic planning, and college preparation for millions of students around the globe.

Introducing Naviance for Elementary School

Begin with the goal in mind

Naviance for Elementary School helps ensure equal exposure to college knowledge and career pathways for all students. Help students understand who they are, discover unique paths, and develop the skills to be successful in school and beyond.

A Comprehensive, Unified Solution

Naviance has the tools that schools and districts need to maximize every student’s potential and improve institutional effectiveness, plus premier services and support to help educators achieve success.

Our Platform

One place for students to identify their strengths and align their interests to long-term college and career goals, plus all the tools schools and district administrators need to monitor progress and track student outcomes.

91%

In 2008 at Yonkers Public Schools, college-going intentions for seniors was 67%. In 2012, after implementing Naviance in partnership with Yonkers Partners in Education (YPIE), close to 91% of seniors applied to colleges and 76% of seniors intended to enroll in four- or two-year colleges.

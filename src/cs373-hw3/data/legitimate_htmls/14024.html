SHAPE THE FUTURE OF AUDIO

Following our mission to Shape the Future of Audio, Sennheiser creates era-defining products like AMBEO 3D products and the Sennheiser HE 1 – products that challenge the status quo in audio and offer profound new ways of experiencing. This is our art. At ‘Shape the Future of Audio’, Sennheiser invites visionary artists to present their interpretation of the Future of Audio with sound projects that redefine the way we experience music and art.

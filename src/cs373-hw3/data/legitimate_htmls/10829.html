Wedding Dresses, Bridesmaid Dresses and Prom Dresses

bridal gowns, bridal party dresses, mother of the bride dresses and more

You can always tell when a bride finds the one. It's an incredibly inspiring moment. And we love it. Whether you want to look classic or modern, glamorous or elegant, we've got gorgeous wedding dresses for you at an amazing price. Let us help you discover your dream dress in our ever-changing designer collections.

Vera Wang Wedding Dresses - Whether your style is romantic, classic or whimsical, Vera Wang is probably the first name that comes to mind when you think of bridal designers. Her revolutionary collections are known around the world for sophisticated drama, feminine detailing and a modern approach to bridal design. If you're looking for unique bridal designs with signature details, you'll fall in love with our exclusive White by Vera Wang collection, including bridal gowns, bridesmaid dresses and accessories.

Melissa Sweet Wedding Dresses - Modern romantic brides inspired by the well-made things of the past are sure to fall under the spell of our Melissa Sweet collection. The graceful gowns incorporate vintage detailing like cascading lace and intricate hand-sewn beading. Polished yet pretty, the designer bridal collection also includes beautiful coordinating veils.

Oleg Cassini Wedding Dresses - When you dream of your big day, do you envision luxurious, elegant details that your guests will remember forever? If so, our Oleg Cassini collection is made just for you. These timeless bridal gowns are designed with opulent details like hand-embroidered embellishments and sparkling crystals. Go for head-to-toe elegance with graceful finishing touches. 		Galina Signature Wedding Dresses - If you love fashion and drama, you'll want every detail of your wedding to be sophisticated and glamorous, starting with your gorgeous gown. The Galina Signature collection features rich, sultry wedding dresses with unforgettable details like lush textures and ultra-dramatic skirt ruffles. Complete your look with vintage inspired accents with a serious wow factor. Free-spirited brides look to express their style in a fun, contemporary way.

Galina Wedding Dresses - If simple luxury is what you're seeking, our Galina collection offers hip wedding dresses that let the real you shine through. Balance an elegantly minimalistic gown with high-impact bridal accessories.

David's Bridal Collection Wedding Dresses - Traditional, all-American brides will fall in love with the David's Bridal collection. These classic bridal gowns are designed in a variety of shapes from slim sheaths to full ball gowns to modern fit-and-flare styles. Flattering details include floaty layers, beautiful beading and color accents. For a look truly fit for a princess, add a shimmery tiara and sparkle-trimmed veil.

For more inspiration on finding your perfect look, shop ALL WEDDING DRESSES.

This is the gateway to different times around the world, closer or further away from the Prime Meridian. It is also the creative home of Unique Timepieces.

Try the new Meeting Planner, a truly international tool. Enjoy and come back often!

Greenwich Mean Time (GMT) is never adjusted backwards or forwards. The UK is on GMT between mid-autumn and the beginning of spring. Currently, it is on daylight saving time (BST), which is GMT+1.

Deluge

The Deluge is a game of chance its free to enter and easy to play. Simply enter a faucet payment address and wait and see if you have won. Only one winner is picked per hour for the hourly deluge and only 1 winner is picked per day on the daily deluge. You can enter the hourly deluge once per hour and the daily deluge once per day. The odds may seem small but the rewards are considerably higher than using the faucets so its worth throwing your payment address into the deluge every so often.

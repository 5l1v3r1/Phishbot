LMS

A learning management system (LMS) is web-based software for administering the delivery of online learning and training. Successful teaching and learning requires a flexible LMS that allows users to easily manage course work, assignments, and evaluations on campus, online, and around the world. Brightspace goes beyond the old “one-size-fits-all” LMS with an easier, more flexible, and smarter learning platform that delivers real results. Brightspace is created by D2L, a leading innovator in education technology.

Learning Management Solutions

Learning solutions facilitate flexible teaching, learning, and evaluation formats such as blended learning, distance education, and flipped classrooms.

LMS Solutions for K-12 Education. Manage course work and assignments, create game-based learning initiatives, and develop digital literacy skills. A flexible learning management system supports online and in-class delivery.

LMS Solutions for Higher Education. Support flexible learning formats for distance learning, continuing education programs, and flipped classrooms. Evaluate performance to drive student retention and increase graduation rates. A LMS helps educators provide a personal learning experience for everyone.

LMS Solutions for Enterprise. Easily administer employee training, onboarding, and facilitate certification programs. Empower field teams with the latest resources while on the go. An easy enterprise LMS solution provides greater employee engagement.

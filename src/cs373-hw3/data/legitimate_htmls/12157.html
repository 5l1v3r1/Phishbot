MyLabsPlus™

MyLabsPlus

  • Learn About
  • Results
  • Support
  • Community

A better way to manage your learning applications and educational technology

Research shows that a more engaged learner is more successful, in the classroom and beyond. That’s why MyLab & Mastering, Pearson’s leading educational technology, is designed to engage and thus help learners reach specific, measurable learning outcomes. MyLabsPlus is the premier enterprise solution that offers a simple way to deliver the engaging experiences that lead to better results across your entire institution.
MyLabsPlus can be connected to your school’s student information system and learning management system, enabling educators and administrators to centrally manage, enroll, and report on student progress across multiple MyLab & Mastering courses. Rich and flexible reporting allows for monitoring of student progress across your courses, department, and institution. And single sign-on from your customized campus portal or your hosted LMS enables easy, convenient access to MyLab & Mastering learning resources on a campuswide scale.

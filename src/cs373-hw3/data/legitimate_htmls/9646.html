Make Games.com your homepage

We see that you are using Chrome as your browser. Here are some quick steps for setting Games.com as your homepage!

  1. Click on the menu button (≡) at the top right and select Settings
  2. Under On startup, click Open a specific page or set of pages
  3. Click Set pages and enter "www.games.com" in the Add a new page field
  4. Under Appearance, check Show Home button and set it to "www.games.com"

The CashStar Difference

Best-In-Class Capabilities

CashStar Commerce enables retailers to create omni-channel experiences across channels, devices and geographies – on a proven cloud platform delivering continuous innovation – with the confidence to scale prepaid programs to unprecedented growth.

Revenue-Optimized Risk Management

CashStar takes a fundamentally different approach to risk, focusing on finding and approving authorized transactions and minimizing false declines. We serve as the merchant of record on a private label basis, assume fraud liability, and manage client risk – ensuring that clients don’t need to deal with fraud prevention, and know that their revenue is as strong as it can be.

Client Success Model

CashStar’s Business model is designed to align with client goals and build partnership to drive prepaid commerce success. Our entire organization – especially our expert Client Success team – is motivated to provided end-to-end support to help retailers create brand differentiation and grow prepaid revenue.

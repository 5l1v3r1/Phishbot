TeamSpeak 3

TeamSpeak 3 offers the ideal voice communication tool for online gaming, education and training, internal business communication, and staying in touch with friends and family.

Our primary focus is delivering a solution that is easy to use, with high security standards, excellent voice quality, and low system and bandwidth usage.

With years of experience and leadership in VoIP since 2001, our engineers have delivered a flexible, powerful, and scalable solution allowing you the ability to customize and tailor your voice communication needs any way you desire.

TeamSpeak 3 SDK

The TeamSpeak 3 Software Development Kit (SDK) is a complete integrated solution for any application requiring crystal clear simultaneous voice communication.

Built on the same technology used to create the incredibly successful TeamSpeak 3, the SDK provides cross-platform support, advanced codec implementation, and complete control over your network infrastructure resulting in stability within your product while minimizing multiple points of failure.

Let us deliver to you a proven solution culminating from years of expertise and experience.

Realist® - The Flexible, Reliable, Property Data Search Engine for Your MLS System

Realist® from CoreLogic® is a public-record database that seamlessly integrates with any web-based multiple listing service (MLS) system to provide in-depth property and ownership data, market information, street and aerial maps, as well as market trends to its users. Realist enhances the capabilities of your MLS system - giving the real estate professionals who rely on your system a data centric, easy to use resource that increases their competitive advantage.

The Property Centric Data You Can Rely On

Realist provides real estate agents and brokers with the perspective necessary to identify, understand and take decisive action based on the data from CoreLogic, the nation's largest provider of property and ownership information. As an MLS, you and your members will benefit from the immediate access Realist provides to 98% of all U.S. real estate transactions updated daily from thousands of public and private sources. You will be able to perform research and make marketing decisions with confidence and extreme accuracy, leveraging data that includes:

  • 99.8 percent of all U.S. property owners
  • 98 percent of all U.S. zip codes
  • 100 percent of all U.S. county, municipal and special tax districts (3,141 counties)
  • 80 percent of mortgage applications
  • 500+ million historical transaction records and data spanning more than 40 years
  • Digital mapping of more than 88 percent of the nation's property parcels

Seamless Integration with Your MLS System

Realist seamlessly integrates with your MLS system, allowing your users to quickly research properties and listings, assess market conditions and trends, value homes, identify comparables, market for buyers and sellers, and create custom exports of information in a single solution. Realist's highly competitive interface and advanced searching capabilities enhance your MLS system functionality while maintaining ease of use, allowing users to:

  • Conduct searches using property and owner characteristics, including owner name, address, street and telephone numbers, tax ID or assessor's parcel number (APN)
  • Create, save and reuse custom search queries as needed
  • Combine MLS listing and Realist data for detailed reports

Powerful Marketing and Sales Tools

Realist's advanced search, mapping and marketing analysis capabilities give your MLS members the tools they need to help achieve their sales and marketing goals. With Realist your members can:

  • Improve direct marketing efforts using dynamic mapping solutions to search and capture property data visually with street maps and aerial overlays
  • Create selling tools, including home value reports and listing kits, with custom reporting options
  • Export real estate data for contact management applications, marketing lists and mailing labels
To learn more about Realist and how it can give you and your members a competitive advantage, view our presentation.

 

Why is Realist Different?

Realist is unlike any other property-record database in the industry. It fuses numerous sources and categories of information into its underlying database of all properties. Only Realist offers the most comprehensive real estate information at the national, regional and local level. You and your members are empowered with flexible, current and reliable data. Benefit from:

  • Comprehensive data updated daily
  • Industry-leading mapping technology with the ability to query your results in a number of ways
  • Complete customization at the MLS and user level
  • Integration with any MLS system
  • Reverse-Link feature which allows you to seamlessly switch between public and MLS records
  • AVM's supplemented with MLS listing information to provide the most accurate home values

 

Property Centric Information

MLS Listing Data + Tax Data = Winning Combination

Realist® from CoreLogic® is your property-centric resource for obtaining a comprehensive overview of a specific property and the neighborhood in which it is located. Realist fuses numerous sources and categories of information into its underlying database of all properties. WIth Realist, you have the ability to conduct a search to explore all of the homes for sale in an area. Then you can narrow your focus to homes that are listed as a short sale. From there, you can instantly identify which homes are near schools, restaurants, and other points-of-interest that your buyers may seek. Only a property-centric system like Realist can empower you to learn about an entire area and then zero-in on the various types of information you need.

Experience the Property-Centric Benefits of Realist

With the property-centric focus of Realist, you will benefit from:

  • Best in class property information which includes tax and assessment, property characteristics, ownership information, sales & mortgage history, plus foreclosure and REO data - all complemented with current trends and market intelligence
  • Combined tax, MLS, and community information presented via a single source solution
  • Instant and integrated access to core MLS information and photos which provides a holistic view of an entire community
  • Community information that supports neighborhood trends, points-of-interest, and lifestyle content
  • Search results presented on the grid and map which allows you to view the results the way you want to

Realist Seamlessly Integrates With Your MLS System

Realist integrates with any MLS system providing members with direct access to ownership records, geographic data, tax and assessment data, sales comparables, foreclosures, neighborhood information and much more. Realist was purpose built for MLS's and its members. The flexibility from Realist enables users to conduct in-depth searches using several property, ownership and MLS listing criteria including: owner name, address, telephone number, tax ID or Assessor's Parcel Number (APN).

  • Boost your search results using more than 30 MLS attributes including property characteristics, listing number, listing status, agent name, broker name and more
  • The Property Detail Report is augmented with your MLS data to view past listings and other data only available within the MLS record
  • The Comparable Report uses integrated tax and MLS data to identify comparables that are currently on the market for an accurate picture of the competition
  • Navigate back to full MLS listing record from Realist effortlessly

 

Advanced Mapping

Advanced Mapping Capabilities For MLS Systems

Realist® from CoreLogic® features advanced mapping functionality that helps you visualize subject properties, comparables, market trends and points-of-interest. Its dynamic interface supports the searching and capturing of property data - including listings, recent sales and foreclosures. The Realist Map lets you layer multiple drawing tools such as polygon, rectangular and circular shapes to identify a geographic area or you can zoom in on a specific property with road, aerial and bird's eye views. Instantly identify if a subject property is located next to a freeway, waterway, school, shopping area or other point-of-interest.

Use the dynamic mapping tools to:

  • View subject and nearby property characteristics
  • View foreclosure activity in the area
  • View sales and listing activity in a neighborhood
  • View thematic maps showing market conditions and trends

View Key Market & Real Estate Trends from All Angles

Analyze and understand how real estate and market trends, coupled with geography can affect you and your business. Realist provides everything you need to realize the benefits of spatial analysis with a single, easy-to-use solution.

  • Mapping is completely integrated with searching - view tax and MLS information (foreclosures, listings and recently sold properties) together on the map
  • Begin your search from a map, view the results, annotate and then print/email for a comprehensive presentation of data
  • Visualize and study market trend information through thematic mapping including median sale price, median home value, sales activity, median square foot, etc

New Insight with Map Visualization

By simply typing an address, you are taken to the correct location on a map where you can provide answers to questions such as:

  • Is the site near a railroad or highway?
  • Are there parks or other facilities nearby?
  • What is the range of home values?
  • Are there good schools nearby?

Using the integrated map, you can visually determine the properties available for sale in any given neighborhood and weigh the relative pros and cons of each location. Use the search tools to locate listings, based on location or attributes. For example, you could choose from among all of your listings the ones that are in a specific school district, a particular price range, or have a minimum number of rooms.

Email reports to home buyers and sellers providing them with informative and helpful analysis that includes:

Professional Reports

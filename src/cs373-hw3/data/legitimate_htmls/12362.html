HONE IN ON THE MOST EFFECTIVE EMAILS & SALES CONTENT.

Build a library of personalized email templates & sales content that you can access right from your inbox. See useful metrics on which email templates & content are getting opened and clicked. Share the best approaches across your team.
LEARN ABOUT TEMPLATES

FIND NEW SOURCES OF INTERESTED PROSPECTS.

Prospects helps you identify the companies who are visiting your site right now. Get deep insight into the number of visitors from each company and the pages they are viewing. Set up daily email digests of exactly the prospects that matter most to each member of your team.

KEEP YOUR CALENDAR FULL WITH HALF THE EFFORT.

Stop the email back and forth and put the power to book meetings in the hands of your prospects. Set your availability in HubSpot Sales and use your personalized booking link to let anyone set an appointment with you in seconds.

CONNECT WITH YOUR PROSPECTS IN A RELEVANT, EFFICIENT WAY.

Sequences makes it easy to tee up a series of email steps to be sent to a prospect over time, right from your inbox. Calling makes it easy to surface the best prospects to reach out to, and connect from your browser without ever touching a phone.

CRM integration means you’ll never manually log another email again.

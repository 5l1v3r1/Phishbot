About SOCCER.COM

Our Mission: To be the world's leading authentic grassroots soccer company, building strong relationships in a vibrant, creative and ethical environment. To inform, inspire and innovate.

Based in Hillsborough, North Carolina, our company was founded in 1984 by the Moylan family to provide American soccer players with equipment and information unavailable anywhere else. We mailed our first catalog, Eurosport, in 1984 and launched our website, SOCCER.COM, in 1994. Since our first soccer ball left the warehouse, we have promoted the game of soccer at all levels and educated players, referees and their families about the game and its equipment.

The SOCCER.COM website showcases an unequaled selection of top-of-the-line soccer merchandise used by world-class players and teams. We carry such top brands as adidas, Asics, Brine, Diadora, Joma, Kelme, Kwik Goal, Lanzera, Lotto, Nike, Pirma, PUMA, Reusch, Uhlsport, Umbro, Under Armour, Veloce, Voit, Warrior and many others. SOCCER.COM is also proud to feature the official gear of top teams such as the US National Team, Brazilian National Team, Manchester United, FC Barcelona, Real Madrid, Chelsea FC and many, many more. We may carry the largest selection of soccer gear, but we also provide service and delivery that is unmatched in the soccer world.

There's one simple reason we are the best soccer company on the planet: we love soccer.

853544 Ninjago Skybound Battle Pack found in the wild

Posted by TheBrickPal, 24 May 2016 22:00

Skybound Battle Pack

853544-1 Ninjago Skybound 2016
©2016 LEGO Group
New sets
9 comments
1186 Views

Today sees the reveal of a new brand store exclusive battle pack based on the Ninjago Skybound subtheme (853544).

The set has so far only been spotted in Europe, but every fan will want to get ahold of it once it is released worldwide as ALL of the four minifigures included are unique to the set. A version of Zane reminiscent of his damaged look during the Tournament of Elements, a special variant of Nya as Samurai X, and two foot soldiers of Nadakhan's sky pirate crew make the lineup!

It should retail for $14.99 or 12.99€ if similar sets are to go by. onikari on Eurobricks owns the set and has provided detailed photography of it here. What do you think?

Review: 60121 Volcano Exploration Truck

Posted by CapnRex101, 23 May 2016 16:30
Set review
30 comments
7912 Views

The Volcano Explorers subtheme dominates the City range this summer and follows the successful examples of the Deep Sea and Arctic sets by taking the action beyond the city limits and into the wilderness.

60121 Volcano Exploration Truck is perhaps the least unusual of the seven sets which have been revealed so far but I was drawn to it thanks to my love for vehicles of this size which are a mainstay of LEGO City. Furthermore, the set is priced at £14.99 or $24.99 so offers a relatively inexpensive taste of the entire Volcano Exploration range as well as a number of new pieces.

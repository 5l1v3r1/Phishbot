INSTANT REPLAYS MADE BY THE FANS

With livestreaming and eSports getting recognition, we wanted to give the unsung heroes of gaming the coolest tool we could think of.

Oddshot works by installing a plugin to your browser, then typing !shot into the livestream chat.

We'll deliver you back a URL of the instant replay of whatever just happened, from there the possibilities are endless.

WIN PRIZES AND STREET CRED

Expect regular best shot competitions. From eSports matches, to new game releases, we'll look through the submissions and pick 10 of our favorites. From there the community votes on who grabbed the best moment.

The winners will take a variety of prizes ranging from game keys, to merchandise from the best competitive teams.

About Musician's Friend

ABOUT MUSICIAN’S FRIEND

Welcome to the Musician’s Friend online store—your destination for the best musical instruments, gear and exclusive content to help you get the sound you’re after. We offer free shipping (on almost everything) from the biggest online selection of musical instruments and equipment including guitars, effects, basses, guitar amplifiers, iOS, keyboards, drums, live sound, DJ, microphones, recording, band and orchestra, folk, accessories, and more. You’ll even find an unmatched selection of open-box instruments and gear that ships worldwide.

To help you choose the instrument that’s right for you, many of our products feature unbiased reviews from Musician’s Friend customers who have first-hand knowledge of the gear you’re interested in. You can also access our experienced customer service team by chatting online, or by calling 877-880-5907. And for even more music know-how, news, reviews and buying guides, visit The HUB.

As the largest online guitar store anywhere in the world, we have all of your favorites from Fender, Gibson, and Taylor including Stratocaster, Telecaster, and Les Paul guitars. If you’re looking to add something truly special to your collection, we take great pride in our hand-curated Private Reserve Guitars. Check out the latest product releases at our NAMM 2016 page. Home to the finest guitars, basses and amps anywhere in the world, the Musician’s Friend PRG collection consists of some truly awesome guitars, including premium, limited edition and collectible models from the world’s top manufacturers.

We have a vast Pro Tools Collection that has every current version and upgrade option to meet your needs. Be sure to check out the new flagship Yamaha Montage Synthesizer and the Martin Ed Sheeran X acoustic-electric guitar, as well as our collection of the new 2016 Gibson USA models now in stock—see photos and choose your exact guitar before it’s gone. See our Featured Products page for more of our latest product releases and special offers. If you are looking for a great gift for the musician in your life, we have an unbeatable assortment of iOS-compatible gear, ukuleles, books, apparel and collectibles and even instruments for children. If you’re not sure which product is right for you, let our friendly and helpful Gear Heads quickly get you the gear you need. Chat online or call us at 877-880-5907.

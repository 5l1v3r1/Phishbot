ABOUT US

Oasis Games Limited was established in 2011. Oasis Games was the first Chinese company to bring online games to overseas markets, spreading into 70 countries and translating games into 16 languages. In 2015, we built a complete game promotion platform in addition to continuously publishing high-quality games.

GAMES

Oasis Games' excellent operation and marketing methods as well as our fine-tuned localization for overseas markets have led to a string of successes in the past few years. Oasis Games has been continually expanding to international markets, where we have managed to build a large fan base. Over 35 games have been brought to overseas markets, and local consumers love the diversity we offer. The games' genres include SRPG, RTS, fantasy adventure, shooters, etc.

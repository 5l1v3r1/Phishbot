Speed up your development lifecycle with an easy, accessible, and effective testing and collaboration tool

Say no to bugs

Bugs are more and more expensive to fix the further along you are in development. Test soon and test often to eliminate pesky bugs before they cause too much trouble (and cost you users.)

Test with your eyes closed, literally

Genymotion integrates with your continuous integration server, like Jenkins or Gradle, so you can automatically run the tests you've written, even when you aren't there - at last a way to work when you're not working.

One platform, many options

The Genymotion emulator has over 3,000 Android configurations to choose from. Developing and testing the best app for the myriad devices out there is now possible and simpler than ever before. Oh, and it’s cheaper and faster too.

Quicker APK deployment than on any actual device

Avoid 10 bugs/ month (on average) and save over $800,000

Genymotion is three times quicker to boot than an actual device

Who is Genymotion for?

Sales Team

I can easily showcase apps on my laptop, project them on a wide screen, make perfect screencasts at full speed to show the best of them.

Helpdesk Operators

Without having the same physical device as my customers, I can simulate the same one and reproduce the behavior of their app step by step.

QA Testers

Fast tests lead to more tests, and more tests lead to better quality. The perfect compliance with the Android standard ensures that all my testing tools will work out of the box.

Developers

I can develop, test and view my apps running instantly on all combinations of large and small devices, low and high def screens, old and new Android versions, at full speed.

Features

Genymotion is 100% compliant with Android APIs because we follow exactly the same rules as every Android device factory. But what we produce isn’t a device, it’s so much more: it’s the perfect Android environment for you to build and test your app.

Vary your GPS location, network quality, or battery charge level without ever leaving your desk. You can even simulate phone calls or text to see how your app will react.

These are just a few of the many features that make Genymotion special.

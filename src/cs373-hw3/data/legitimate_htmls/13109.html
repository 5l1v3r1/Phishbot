  1. Choose the advertising option that works best for you: subscription fee or pay-per-booking
  2. Set up payments to make it easy for travelers to book securely online using their debit or credit card

Booking a Property

  1. Search using your destinations and dates
  2. Look for the Book Now option or click Enquire Now and your request will be sent directly to the owner or the owner’s property manager.
  3. Pay with your credit or debit card if the owner accepts Online Payments.  If not, before sending any payment, you should always call the telephone number on the listing and speak with the owner or property manager to confirm your booking and payment details.

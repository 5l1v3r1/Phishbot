Genes for nose shape found

Genes that drive the shape of human noses have been identified by a UCL-led study. The four genes mainly affect the width and ‘pointiness’ of noses which vary greatly between different populations.

Open City Documentary Festival programme announced

The festival takes place over six days from 21-26 June and brings the best in contemporary, international documentary alongside filmmaker Q&As, special events, workshops and parties to the UCL campus and cinemas across London. BAFTA TV Award for Britain’s Forgotten Slave Owners

BBC documentary Britain’s Forgotten Slave Owners, presented by David Olusoga in collaboration with UCL History's Legacies of British Slave-ownership research project, has won the 'specialist factual' category at the 2016 BAFTA TV Awards.

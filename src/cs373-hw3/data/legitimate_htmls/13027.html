French Natural Beauty Products

For over 30 years, L'OCCITANE has drawn inspiration from French and Provençal art de vivre and techniques to create natural skincare and bodycare products dedicated to your beauty and to your well-being, with our aromatherapy collection. Our eco-responsible effective products are rich in natural active ingredients and essential oils, which are often organic and always of controlled origin.

From skincare to bath and hair care, our products feature luxurious natural perfume and textures from ingredients like pure shea butter, french lavender, almond, verbena and immortelle!

We know what are your beauty needs and we have a solution for them all: anti aging creams, eye creams, facial cleansers, hand and foot creams… Just browse our website and discover your true natural beauty.

Want to know more about Provence ? Discover our magazine with plenty of tips, recipes, interviews and much more for lovers of Provence !

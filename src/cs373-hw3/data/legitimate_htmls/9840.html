Welcome to enviat.com Network Tracking Services

Clients use the enviat.com domain as part of a service to protect users from harmful activity, to provide value for the media ecosystem, and as a quality signal for surfacing relevant, interesting content.

Why do I see traffic from *.enviat.com?

Your site may have received visitors from an embedded link or relevant traffic source from somewhere within our network.

What is the purpose of an *.enviat.com link?

Our link service allows site owners and traffic holders to monitor traffic sources while maintaining control over the end destination.

Our link service measures information such as how many times a link has been clicked, which is an important quality signal in determining how relevant and interesting content is to the visitor.

Having a network tracked link also protects users from malicious sites that engage in spreading malware, phishing attacks, and other harmful activity. An enviat.com link is checked against a list of potentially dangerous sites. Users are warned with error messages when clicking on potentially harmful URLs. If you think a link is being used for malicious activitity, please get in touch.

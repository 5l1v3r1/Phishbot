  • We Create New Industries

    Drexel, together with MIT, Cornell and UCF have founded Advanced Functional Fabrics of America, a venture that will transform the nation’s textile manufacturing by expanding the use of advanced fibers and functional fabrics.

    Learn More

  • We see the big picture

    At Drexel, we encourage our students to embrace the creative spirit. You’ll find inspiration everywhere — in classes, with our performing arts groups, on campus, and within our galleries.

    Learn More

  • We want the best

    With an entrepreneurial drive, groundbreaking research, a world-renowned cooperative education program, and an eye towards reinventing University City through innovative design, Drexel is attracting the best and the brightest minds to campus.

    Learn More

  • We love our Philadelphia

    The City of Brotherly Love is so much more than pavement and skyscrapers. Every block you walk is different from the rest. Parks, playgrounds, theaters, restaurants — there are so many things to do and see in this historic landmark we call home.

    Learn More

  • We have the connections

    Through cooperative education, Drexel students can gain real-world experience with the leaders of today, build a strong network, and explore different career paths before graduation. Preview what’s possible with Drexel Co-op.

    Learn More

  • We jump into action

    Whether we’re playing on Division I teams or planning on-campus concerts, Drexel students are actively involved in our 300+ clubs and organizations. We’re passionate about everything we do, and there are plenty of options available.

    Learn More

  • We take field trips

    Classroom learning is just one component of a Drexel education in the sciences. We believe in field experience, early and often. Led by our expert faculty, you’ll get started in the field or laboratory from day one and begin making a difference.

    Learn More

  • We will go anywhere

    Dragons can be found everywhere around the world taking on today’s global challenges and opportunities. At Drexel, you can explore numerous paths of international study and gain a new perspective.

    Learn More

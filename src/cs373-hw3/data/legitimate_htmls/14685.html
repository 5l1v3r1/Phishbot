Getting Started

Get going with HockeyApp easily. Just sign up and start tracking crashes and distributing app builds in three simple steps:

1

Download SDK

Download the respective client SDK for your platform. Be it iOS, Android, Windows Phone, or Mac.

2

Integrate SDK

Integrate the SDK using either one of our client apps or manually with just a few clicks.

3

Distribution

Upload your app build to HockeyApp and you're all set. Your testers can now download and install your app.

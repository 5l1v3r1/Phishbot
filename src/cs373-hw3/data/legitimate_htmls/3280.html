 Welcome to BudsGunShop.com
 

We have guns for sale at BudsGunShop.com. Our site is automatically updated every 20 minutes (24/7) by computer to reflect current inventory and prices.

If you have questions please read our Shipping & Payment Information, Privacy Notice, or Conditions of Use before calling or emailing us. Please look over our inventory before calling or emailing for price and availability of an item. If the item quantity is zero, or is displayed as a "Wish List" item, then we do not currently have the item in stock.

All items advertised on www.budsgunshop.com or www.budspolicesupply.com are NOT necessarily in stock or on display at our retail store. To check price and availability of an item in the retail store, please call 859-368-0419.

Local customers can buy online and choose in store pickup during checkout. This fast and convenient option insures that your purchase will be ready and waiting when you arrive at our store. Our retail store location does not charge customers for transferring a firearm purchased from BudsGunShop.com. However, all in-store pick-ups will be subject to the 6% Kentucky state sales tax.

Notification

From the 25th until the 28th of May, Airports in Japan will enhance security measures at screening checkpoints in support of the G7 Summit at Ise-Shima.
Passengers are strongly encouraged to arrive early at airports to allow sufficient time for check-in and the enhanced security screening processes. Thank you for your cooperation. 

Check-in counter at Hong Kong

Please note that Peach check-in counter will be moved to Terminal 1/ E counter effective from March 27, 2016.
However, check-in for MM68/March 27 will be held at Terminal 2/ P counter, please pay attention.

 

Regarding Electric Vehicles Containing Lithium ion Batteries

Peach prohibits transport of electric vehicles containing lithium ion batteries such as hoverboards, Segways, Electric Bicycles etc. for safety reason.
※This regulation may not be applied to battery-powered wheelchairs or other similar mobility aids that are used by passengers with limited mobility.

 

Rules for Carry-on Baggage

To ensure flight safety, passengers may only take on board one bag and one personal belonging e.g. handbag, umbrella etc. Total must not exceed two pieces.
Any item which exceeds the size and weight regulations for carry-on baggage must be checked in at the baggage counter for a fee.
Please check the details here.

What does it do?
The Old Reader allows you to subscribe to your favorite sites so you can stay in the know. Connect with friends to share and discuss articles you love. Send links to your favorite social networks, or save them to Pocket, Instapaper or Readability.
What does this service cost?
The Old Reader is free for up to 100 feeds. We also offer a premium version for $3/month that has the following features:
  • No Advertisements
  • Full-text search
  • Faster feed refresh times
  • Up to 500 Subscriptions *
  • 1 year of post storage **
  • Instapaper and Readability integration
  • Early access to new features
Can I transfer my subscriptions?
Yep! Importing an OPML export from another service is a breeze.
How do I find my friends?
Connect your account to Facebook or Google and we'll see who else is here (we don't ever store any of your Facebook or Google contact data).
Do you have a mobile app?
Yes! Our fantastic community of developers have built apps for just about any platform. We have a great API which allows anyone to write an app for The Old Reader. Check out our apps page to see all of the great apps available.
Are you going to send junk to my social networks or hijack my links?
Absolutely not. We don't believe that's the right thing to do. You're in complete control of what you share to your social networks. When you share it, your friends will get a direct link to see the post, not some link to our site.

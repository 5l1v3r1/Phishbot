'I've been a strong supporter of the online scene; I've always been pushing it so we can get to this level now' Alex Valle sits in on Best of 3

Posted by Tony 'grumblebundle' Moore — 3 hours, 17 minutes ago

Earlier this week, Alex Valle interviewed on the latest episode of Best of 3, and he came prepared to answer the tough questions about the Capcom Pro Tour online ranking tournament coming up this weekend.

The brackets for the tournament filled up not too long after it was announced, and with such a short time period between the announcement and the start date, some players had concern about how things were going to be run.

"Initially we weren't going to seed anybody, but since it's so far into the Pro Tour, it only makes sense to seed the current ranking players..." Valle says, before going on to explain how lag disputes and other rulings will work.

Beyond that, the interview covers a wide variety of topics including partnerships to bring high quality production to the online regionals, Red Bull Vanguard, plans for SoCal Regionals, and more.

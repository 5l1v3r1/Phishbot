Maccabi FOX Tel Aviv was one of three teams to take a 2-0 series lead in their best-of-five Israeli League quarterfinals on Monday . Maccabi FOX routed Bnei Herzliya 51-68 behind a balanced team effort led by Trevor Mbakwe's 13 points and 9 rebounds.

One Team starred at Final Four in Berlin

To top off a season during which its clubs and players dedicated more time, effort and attention than ever before to help at-risk groups in their communities, Euroleague Basketball's corporate social responsibility programme One Team was in the spotlight again at the 2016 Turkish Airlines Final Four in Berlin with a series of activities that raised awareness to help groups in danger of social exclusion.

About Fine Art America

FineArtAmerica.com has revolutionized the way that artwork is bought and sold around the world.   With a few clicks, artists and photographers can upload their images to FineArtAmerica.com, set their prices for all of our available products, and sell those products to a global audience of online art buyers.   Fine Art America handles the logistics of fulfilling each order.   We take care of the printing, framing, matting, packaging, shipping, collecting payments from our buyers, and sending profits to our sellers.   Each product is custom manufactured at one of our global production facilities and delivered to our buyers with a 30-day money-back guarantee.   We've been in business since 2006 and are the largest online art site in the world!

In addition to providing an online marketplace and fulfillment service, Fine Art America also provides artists and photographers with sales and marketing tools to help simplify and accelerate their careers.   These tools allow our sellers to set up branded web stores, sell prints on Facebook, sell prints on Amazon, create e-newsletters, and much more.

Hundreds of thousands of artists and photographers have uploaded million of images to FineArtAmerica.com - with thousands of new members and images being added each week.   Each month, millions of visitors stop by FineArtAmerica.com to purchase prints and other products from this ever-expanding collection and to socialize and network with the world's greatest artists and photographers.

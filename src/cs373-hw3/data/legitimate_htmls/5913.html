SPOTLIGHTING IU SUCCESS

IU Alum addresses 2016 graduates

Indiana University alumnus Jamie Hyneman, a special effects expert and inventor best known as co-host and executive producer of the popular TV show “MythBusters,” was the featured speaker at undergraduate commencement. He has an IU degree in Russian language and linguistics through the Individualized Major Program in the College of Arts and Sciences. Hyneman shared his experiences on the show and discussed the nature of success.

Watch Jamie's speech

Earning their stripes

It’s not uncommon to find students on campus showing off their candy-striped apparel. However, Professor Jane Matranga’s fashion design students had to think outside of the stripes when she challenged them to create their own Hoosier apparel in her draping class. After watching her students overcome issues with the fabric and fit of their designs, she was excited to see the results.

Watch the video

Brewing solutions for sustainability

Many people don’t consider the process that goes into brewing beer, yet alone its impacts on the environment. The 2020 Sustainability Scholars Program is looking to change that by pairing undergraduates with faculty mentors to conduct sustainability research. As part of the study, a SPEA research team is exploring sustainable solutions for the brewing industry in order to encourage the adoption of more environmentally friendly practices.

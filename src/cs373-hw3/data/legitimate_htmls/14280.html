About Tobi

Established in 2007, Tobi is an online design label based in Los Angeles. With LA as the source of our inspiration, we bring the casual, cool, and sexy attitude of the city into every one of our products. Every day we are designing the trendiest fashion clothes straight from our design studio and selling them exclusively on our online clothing store at tobi.com. In just a few years, Tobi has become one of the top women's clothing websites and a staple in the world of online fashion with over 1.5 million Tobi babes in 100+ countries flaunting their favorite Tobi pieces. Shop all the latest designs in cute dresses -- like pretty maxi dresses, sexy bodycon dresses, and sequin dresses -- or other cute clothes like flirty crop tops, essential bodysuits, and chic jumpsuits & rompers. Happy online shopping! #shoptobi

MYTHERESA.COM – ONLINE SHOP FOR LUXURY AND DESIGNER FASHION

Discover mytheresa.com, the epicentre of runway highlights and exclusive seasonal offerings from New York, London, Paris and Milan’s luxury fashion designers. The mytheresa edit features clothing, bags, shoes and accessories from more than 180 international luxury designer brands such as Balenciaga, Burberry, Alexander McQueen, Diane von Furstenberg, Saint Laurent, and Christian Louboutin, as well as key pieces from on-trend labels like Acne Studios, Isabel Marant, Mulberry, Roger Vivier, Proenza Schouler, By Malene Birger, plus many more. The mytheresa.com fashion and designer clothing website is updated with a total of over 500 new arrivals a week from the latest collections, cementing it as the heartbeat of today’s fashion trends.

A UNIQUE SHOPPING EXPERIENCE

  • More than 180 of the world’s hottest designers
  • 500 New Arrivals a week from the latest runway collections
  • Exclusive womenswear fashion
  • Weekly editorials, presenting the newest trends and style inspiration

SERVICE AND QUALITY

  • Authentic products
  • Fast, reliable delivery to over 120 countries within 72 hours
  • E-commerce competence 
  • Free returns and exchanges

The roots of the mytheresa.com online store are grounded in the successful ethos of the European luxury store, THERESA, which has enjoyed the trust and loyalty of top designer labels since 1987. Every season our team travels all over the world to the foremost fashion capitals to select the products that fit the mytheresa.com philosophy of elegant, contemporary fashion.

Our comprehensive product range extends from Chloé’s ‘Marcie’ bag, Isabel Marant’s ‘Bobby’ wedge sneakers and coveted Moncler coats, to Diane von Furstenberg dresses, Miu Miu belts and Tabitha Simmons boots.

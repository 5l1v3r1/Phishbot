OFFSHORE AND INTERNATIONAL COMPANY FORMATION, TRUSTS AND FOUNDATIONS FOR WEALTH PROTECTION AND INTERNATIONAL BUSINESS

OCRA Worldwide establishes, manages and administers companies, trusts and foundations to meet our client' specific objectives. Broadly these objectives include corporate re-structuring, wealth protection, tax reduction, crossborder investment and foreign market entry.

As a leading global corporate and trust service provider established in 1975, we offer our clients more than 35 years' experience and employ more than 350 staff across our network of 19 offices worldwide. We have established in excess of 200,000 companies and trusts and provide ongoing administration for many of these entities on behalf of our clients.

Our compliance standards are amongst the best in the industry, and where required, we are a licensed Corporate and Trust Service Provider.

Contact us today for a confidential discussion without obligation on how we can help you meet your objectives.

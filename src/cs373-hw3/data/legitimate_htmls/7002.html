Ertharin Cousin, Executive Director of the United Nations World Food Programme, To Serve as 2016 Rensselaer Commencement Speaker

Honorary degree recipients Dr. David Skorton, Secretary of the Smithsonian Institution, and Dr. Steven Weinberg, acclaimed physicist and Nobel laureate, will join Cousin for the President’s Commencement Colloquy on the eve of Commencement on May 27. Led by President Shirley Ann Jackson, they will participate in a discussion titled “The Unity Underlying Diversity: Multi-cultural, Intellectual, and Geographic.”

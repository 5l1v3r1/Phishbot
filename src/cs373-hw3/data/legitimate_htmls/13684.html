HOW VIP
MEMBERSHIP WORKS


The choice to shop is always yours! If you need more time to shop or have decided not to purchase, click the 'Skip the Month' button in your account by the 5th of each month and you won't be charged. You can 'Skip' as many months as you want!

If you don't shop or 'Skip the Month' by the 5th, your credit card will be charged $39.95 on the 6th, and placed into your account as a credit until you cancel.

You may cancel your VIP Membership at any time by calling our Client Services at 1-888-508-1888, available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
*Free shipping for VIPs on orders over $39.00 within the contiguous United States only, excluding APO/FPO addresses.

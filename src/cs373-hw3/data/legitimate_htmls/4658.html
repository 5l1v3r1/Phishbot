Welcome to IngDan

We're a one-stop supply chain centre for online-to-offline services, linking entrepreneurs
with the right suppliers and supporting them from inception through to fruition.

Learn More

The ‘Internet of Things’(IoT) is a game changer in our lives.
Yet it’s not about the ‘things’ – it’s about the people that use them.

That’s why we wake up every morning and do what we do best: help you realize your IoT product dreams. Our passion for people drives us, it helps that we’re pretty damn good at what we do. Our passion for people drives us, that’s how we have become the leading supply chain and innovation platform in the world.

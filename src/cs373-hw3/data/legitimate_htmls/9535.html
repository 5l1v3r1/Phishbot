    FAQ

    How to contact the ministry?

    Ministry of Foreign Affaires - Riyadh Tel 009661 4055000 / 4067777Fax. 009661 4030645 P.O.Box 55937 Postal Code 11544 Ministry of Foreign Affaires Jeddah branch 0096626690900 Ministry of Foreign Affaires Dammam branch 0096638683310

    How can I find the addresses of embassies and diplomatic missions of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia abroad?

    You can get the addresses and contact information of Saudi Arabia embassies and diplomatic missions abroad by visiting MOFA's website, under the section of "Saudi embassies and diplomatic missions: http://www.mofa.gov.sa/SITES/MOFAEN/SAUDIMISSIONSABROAD/SAUDIEMBASSIESABROAD

    How often does MOFA update its website?

    The website is updated on a daily basis.

    How can I obtain a visa for Saudi Arabia?

    Obtaining a visa to enter Saudi Arabia should be through the diplomatic missions abroad or the entry points according to instructions or through MOFA's website under the "consular services" located at the home page, where there is an electronic application to be filled and submitted .And for more information about visas you can visit the page of "consular services" at our website.

    How can I send a question or an opinion regarding foreign policy?

    You can either send your question or opinion via email to the following address:info@mofa.gov.sa, or via postal mail to the following address:Ministry of Foreign Affairs - RiyadhPO Box 55,937 ZIP 11544th

    How can I joined the Ministry of Foreign Affairs?

    You can join the ministry of Foreign Affairs by applying for a competitive examination held annually on the basis of vacancies available, and the required specialties, after being announced in local newspapers, and the ministry's website.

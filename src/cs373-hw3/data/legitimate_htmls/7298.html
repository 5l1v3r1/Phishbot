Empyrion Galactic Survival Servers!

  

Empyrion Galactic Survival

Nitrado is very pleased to welcome Empyrion Galactic survival to the Nitrado cloud enabling you! to create your very own galaxy.

Empyrion is an exciting 3D open world true sandbox survival game situated in space where you can make your own bases, explore, change the terrain, fly and fight in a hostile galaxy full of dangers and surprises.

Each planet is unique and to survive you need to adapt to the environment, there are also different gravity, climates & weather conditions for you and your ships to adjust to.

To survive you will need to find oxygen and food otherwise your time in space will be short, Water and ice planets can supply you with H20 and with the right equipment you can grow your own food in base.

If you decide you want to start digging a tunnel to make a underground base to protect you and your friends then you can, Space is the limit with Empyrion!

 

 

Given the increasingly desperate situation on Earth, the "United Council of Humanity" decided to focus the remaining resources on Earth on a unified effort to build a fleet of starships with the aim of establishing contact with the mysterious aliens and finding Empyrion.

Finally, in the year 2473, the fleet of starships is ready and you are the first commander of the frigate "Helios". After a terrible engine issue and a tough crash which completely destroys the shop and kills the crew.

You and your friends are the only survivors. Find a way to leave this hostile planet and uncover the mysteries of Empyrion. Save the Earth before it is too late!

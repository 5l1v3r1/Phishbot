Loaded. Not bloated.

Ticksy gives you all the tools you need—and nothing you don't—to provide top notch customer support.

  • Public & Private Tickets

    Public tickets are like an online forum; anyone can reply! Private tickets are just between you and your customers (public tickets are optional).

  • Articles & Documentation

    Create text documents and assign them to different categories to create a collection of articles; great for online documentation.

  • Company Branded

    Choose your own subdomain, add a logo and customize your colors to match your brand for a seamless customer experience.

  • Time-Saving Features

    Keyboard shortcuts, quick-loading pages, one-click access to important features and more. Ticksy keeps things efficient!

  • Email & Push Notifications

    Email notifications are sent out for each new ticket and comment, and support agents have the option of using Pushover for instant push notifications.

  • Multilingual

    You can display your system in several different languages. Your support agents and customers have the option to override that setting from their profile.

  • Sleek & Intuitive

    Crafted by the some of the best UI/UX designers in the business, Ticksy gives you an incredibly intuitive and powerful interface.

  • Integrations

    Ticksy also integrates with things like Olark (for built-in live chat), Easy Digital Downloads and Envato Market (to verify purchases).

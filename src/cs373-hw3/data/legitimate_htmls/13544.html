10,000

If a petition gets 10,000 signatures, the government will respond

  1. We demand a Public Inquiry into the UK Government's culture of lying to voters

    The government responded

    The Government refutes the assertions in this petition which do not reflect how this Government operates. This Government is one of the most transparent in the world.

    Read the response in full

  2. We call on the Government to commit to a new national stroke strategy

    The government responded

    Action is being taken to ensure we continue to make progress on stroke. The stroke strategy remains valid and as implementation continues, there are no current plans to renew it.

    Read the response in full

  3. A change in prime minister should trigger a general election.

    The government responded

    As set out in the Cabinet Manual, when a Prime Minister resigns from his position and his MPs have a majority in the Commons, it is for the party or parties in government to identify a successor.

    Read the response in full

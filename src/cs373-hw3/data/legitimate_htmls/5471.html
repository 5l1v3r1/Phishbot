KNOW WHICH SITES TO TRUST!

Web of Trust (WOT) is a free browser extension that tells you which websites you can trust.
Our community & technology power the service that increases your internet safety.

  • WOT Protects You

    Ensure your internet safety. WOT protects you against scams, malware, rogue webstores and dangerous links

  • Know Who to Trust

    Use WOTs add-on and website to check for site reputation and ratings

  • Fast, Easy, Free

    Secure a simple and safe browsing experience. Our add-on doesn’t slow your browser, it’s easy to use and free

  • Warning screen.
    Know what to expect.

    If you land on a site that has poor reputation based on user ratings, WOT shows you a warning. WOT guides you, but the final decision is yours: you can skip the warning and enter the site, or return to the previous page.

  • Search results.
    Separate wheat from the chaff.

    Reputation icons are also shown next to links on search engine results, social media platforms, webmail, and other popular sites. You can choose where from the add-on's settings.

  • Reputation icons guide you.

    When the WOT add-on is installed, you will see a small doughnut shaped icon next to your browser's address bar. The icon shows you the site's rating: green indicates a trustworthy site, yellow tells you to be cautious, and red indicates potential danger.

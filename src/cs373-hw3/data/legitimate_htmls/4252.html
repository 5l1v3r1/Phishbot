“What ingredients are in your coconut oil?”

The only ingredient in Nutiva's coconut oil is…organic, unrefined coconut oil. Nutiva's coconut oil comes from organic coconuts harvested from a small farm in the Philippines. As soon as the coconuts are harvested, the flesh is scooped out by hand and cold-pressed into coconut oil. Unlike other brands, Nutiva's coconut oil is never bleached, deodorized, or refined.

“What nutrients are in coconut oil?”

Coconut oil is full of healthy fats, including lauric acid, a medium-chain triglyceride. This healthy fat may be beneficial to weight loss, can help lower cholesterol, and provides the body with a direct source of energy.

“Is your coconut oil certified organic?”

Yes, Nutiva's coconut oil is certified organic. This means no pesticides or genetically modified organisms were used in the farming or production of the coconuts used to make this oil.

Connecting to your U-verse Home Network

LIVE Channel Availability

Some LIVE channels may not be available because:

  • Programming has been temporarily blacked out for your area.
  • Your U-verse TV package does not include that channel.
    Upgrade now to watch on TV and online.
  • You are not signed in to Uverse.com
  • Your laptop or desktop computer must be connected to your U-verse Home
    Network for you to watch the channel. Get home network support.
    • To connect to your U-verse Home Network you may need to adjust
      your firewall settings.

For more information, please read the Uverse.com Live Channels FAQ.

In-Home-Only LIVE Channels

Due to contractual agreements, some LIVE channels are only available
for online viewing if you are connected to your U-verse home network
and have the LIVE Player Plug-in installed.

For more information, please read the Uverse.com Live Channels FAQ.

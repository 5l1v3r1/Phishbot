Why are equity delivery trades free?

As our name, “Zerodha” (zero+rodha / zero barriers) suggests, we've always wanted to make broking as painless as possible for retail investors. Along with our technological innovations, we hope brokerage free investments will help us grow Indian capital markets by attracting more investors.

How does Zerodha make money then?

  • F&O and intraday equity trades are still charged at a flat Rs 20 per executed order as before.
  • We'll soon be starting margin lending for equity delivery.
  • We have launched Kite trading APIs for institutions who will in turn subsidise our retail investors
  • We hope that this initiative will help grow our client base and business.

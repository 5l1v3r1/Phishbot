Import Export Statistics

India's trade deficit between 18 Apr and 18 May stood at USD 11.45 Billion. During this period India imported goods worth USD 26.19 Billion and exported goods worth USD 14.74 Billion.

17.9% imports were from China followed by United States and Saudi Arabia which accounted for 6% and 5.2% of imports respectively.

Top destination for Indian exports was United States which accounted for 13.3% of exports followed by United Arab Emirates and United Kingdom which accounted for 10% and 5.1% of exports respectively.

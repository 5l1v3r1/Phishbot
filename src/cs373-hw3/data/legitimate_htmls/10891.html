View Freedom Rewards Loyalty Points

FreedomRewardz is a loyalty program initiated by State Bank Group for its debit card holders to reward them with points accumulated over POS/e-commerce transactions, and redeeming the same for a wide range of consumer products. State Bank Group customers are rewarded for every transaction they make using their Debit card. Points can be earned when shopping both in India and overseas.

  • You can now view your Freedom Rewards Loyalty Points:
  • Step 1: Logon to https://www.sbtonline.in/ with your userid and password.
  • Step 2: Select 'Freedom Rewardz' under the 'My Accounts' tab. Two tabs are displayed 'Loyalty Points' and 'Statement'. Default tab displayed is Loyalty Points.
  • Step 3: Details of your accounts are displayed in a tabular format under the heading - SB/CA/OD Account Number, Point Balance, Transactions.
  • Step 4: Corresponding to each account number, and point balance, a hyperlink 'Click here for last 10 transactions' is provided. On clicking this link, the account summary for the last ten transactions is displayed containing details of the transaction like Date, Description, Earned Points and Redeemed Points.
  • Step 5: On clicking the 'Statement' tab, the details of your accounts in a tabular format under the heading - Account No / Nick Name, Account Type and Branch, is displayed. When the radio button related to the corresponding account number is selected, the customer will be required to choose the date range of the statement required, and the appropriate options to download it from 'View', 'Download in Excel Format', 'Download in PDF format'.

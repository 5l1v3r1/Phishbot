    Spring Tavern Heroes Tournament this Weekend!

    This weekend is going to be home to plenty of Hearthstone fun over in Europe. If you are able to attend any of the Fireside Gathering events listed below, DO IT! Not only can you play some awesome Hearthstone but you can also get a Power Core Card Back.

    #ESPORTS

    Quote from Blizzard
    Innkeepers from across Europe and as far as South Africa have heard the call for more Hearthstone! From May 13-15, we’ll be celebrating a weekend of card-slinging, and you can get involved in the fun at a number of new locations.

    The action begins on Friday, May 13, as players battle it out in the Spring Season Tavern Heroes Tournament. The top 32 finishers will then join 128 of Europe’s best-ranked Hearthstone players in the Spring Preliminary on May 14 and 15.

    All of the below-listed venues will be open to the public, so come along and watch the action unfold, have a duel or two with other fans, and enjoy some of the side events lined up for attendees. Many venues are still hosting Tavern Heroes Qualifiers, so you can try to earn a place at the Tavern Hero Tournament on May 13—check out our Fireside Gatherings page to find one near you.

    Livestream Details

    Unable to attend in person? No problem! The event will be livestreamed on the official Hearthstone Twitch channel in English. Streams will also be available in several other languages:

    English: Twitch – PlayHearthstone
    French: Twitch – MilleniumTVHS
    French: Twitch – HearthstoneFR
    Polish: Twitch – Gnimsh
    Russian: Twitch – Starladder_HS_ru  
    Spanish: Twitch – OGSeries

    Stream Times:

    • Saturday, May 14 – Spring Preliminary Day 1 – 14:00-22:00 CEST
    • Sunday, May 15 – Spring Preliminary Day 2 – 14:00-22:00 CEST

    Official Casters

    These are the official casters for the event:

    • Brian Kibler

    Additional Events and Rewards for Attendees

    In addition to showing and hosting the Tavern Hero Tournament and Spring Preliminary from May 13–15, the venues listed above will also be holding various side events for attendees. That means loot and prizes will be available, so be sure to join us in person if you can!

    Disenchant Value Adjustments Reset on May 10th

    This is a public service announcement. The classic cards which received changes with Whispers of the Old Gods can only be disenchanted for full crafting value until May 10th PDT (GMT -7). Those cards are as follows:

    • Ancient of Lore
    • Force of Nature
    • Keeper of the Grove
    • Ironbeak Owl
    • Big Game Hunter
    • Blade Flurry
    • Knife Juggler
    • Leper Gnome
    • Arcane Golem
    • Molten Giant
    • Master of Disguise

    It is our recommendation that you disenchant all these cards if you are not currently using them. You can always hang on to the dust to craft them again if need be, but if you truly don't need them you're better off using that dust for other cards you'd like to play with.

    New Site Feature: Card Audio

    A much requested feature that the community has been asking for is to be able to visit a card page and hear the in-game audio files, and now you can!

    To check it out simply head on over to a card page and check out the Card Sounds section below the flavor text and click on each button to hear the sounds. We'll be updating this when new expansions come out along with our game data import process so you won't have to wait to hear the sounds of new cards any longer!

    Click on the banner below to check out Yogg's card page to play with his sounds.

    Innkeeper Alpha Update - Deck Importing!

    Please be aware. This is the Alpha version of Innkeeper and not only will things break, but they may also be incomplete. Only install it if you're up the the challenge of dealing with bugs and providing us feedback!

    We've recently released an alpha patch to our alpha testing version of Innkeeper which brings a much requested feature to the client - Deck Importing! You can now import decks from HearthPwn into Hearthstone which makes playing new decks so much easier!

    We're looking to get this feature tested thoroughly before moving it to the Innkeeper Beta and could use your feedback. In addition to deck importing, there's a few other fun changes that we've got on Alpha currently..

    Alpha Features

    • Overlay has been updated - Innkeeper face removed, now shows your deck name.
    • Card names on overlay are now displayed in your client's language and not just English.
    • Overlay will now hide if you tab out of Hearthstone. (We will be adding a configurable setting in a future update to disable this)
    • New overlays to track C'Thun stats and Yogg spell casts.
    • Made progress with "Waiting on Hearthstone" and "Stuck at Loading..." issues.

    Must Read Threads for Alpha Testers

    • READ ME - Innkeeper Alpha (Download Alpha Branch Here)
    • Alpha History (Update Changelog)
    • Alpha Testing Forum (For Bug Reports!)

Donations Acceptance Program

The CBP Donations Acceptance Program is pleased to announce its new process for accepting and evaluating proposals that are valued at $3 million or less and are of minimal size, scope and complexity.

The Small-Scale Donation Proposal Process is a more streamlined and simplified avenue for stakeholders interested in investing in and expediting small-scale, high impact border infrastructure, technology and other related improvements.

Congressional Resources

The Office of Congressional Affairs serves as the single point of contact within CBP for all communications between CBP and Congress. OCA is committed to addressing any question or concern Members of Congress or their staffs may have related to CBP’s complex mission.

Spotlight Destination
Kyoto, Japan: Located in the center of Japan, Kyoto is definitely a must-see place in Japan. A surprising number of historical temples and shrines, including 17 UNESCO World Heritage sites, as well as the Kyoto Imperial Palace, make Kyoto one of the world’s top destinations, which allows guests to feel its tradition and elegance. It also boasts Japanese style inns, called Ryokan, with a high standard of hospitality and fine Kyoto cuisine. No other city in the world comes close to Kyoto.

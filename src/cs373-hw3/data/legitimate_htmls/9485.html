EasyHits4U promotes your ads to our members. We have delivered billions of ad views and site visits to our members since 2003. Free and easy to use, EasyHits4U is one of the most prominent internet marketing programs on the web with over 790,000 members.


How it works

For every site you visit, you will receive one visitor back to your link. There's no limit to free visits you can receive per day — 10, 100, or even 1000.

If you have no time to view sites, you can purchase visits right after signup!

How you can benefit

In addition to our free promotional services and tools you will also receive:

• $0.30 for 1000 pages viewed;
• $0.10 for every person you refer;
• 40% from purchases made by your first level referrals.

Easy networking

Connect and communicate with other members, receive free yet optional reviews for your own ads.

You receive 10% of hits earned by your direct referrals, 5% of hits earned by people referred by your referrals, and so on through five tiers.

Marketing tools

Easy to use, powerful and effective tools to create mini pages and site rotators are included. Even in free plan!

Gmail Users
To make sure that you receive emails from us please follow these instructions:
  1. Download the CSV file (“comma-separated list”) from us to your hard drive: EasyHits4U CSV (right-click "Save as…")

  2. Log into your Gmail account, click on the “Contacts” menu option, then click on the “More” link in the top menu, then click on the “Import...” link in the submenu.

  3. Select CSV file above from your hard drive in the new popup.

  4. Click on the “Import” button and you are done. Now you will not lose any e-mail messages from us.


Yahoo Users
To make sure that you receive emails from us please follow these instructions:
  1. Download the CSV file (“comma-separated list”) from us to your hard drive: EasyHits4U CSV (right-click "Save as…")

  2. Log into your Yahoo mailbox, click on the “Contacts” menu option, then click on the “Import Contacts” button.

  3. Choose Others option.

  4. Then choose "A desktop email program (Outlook, AppleMail, etc..." option.

  5. Select a file: Choose one of the downloaded vCard files from your hard drive.

  6. Click on the “Import” button and you are done. Now you will not lose any e-mail messages from us.
    Repeat this step for two other vCard files clicking on the "Import more" button.


Outlook Users
To make sure that you receive emails from us please follow these instructions:
  1. Download the CSV file (“comma-separated list”) from us to your hard drive: EasyHits4U CSV (right-click "Save as…")

  2. Log into your Outlook account, click on the “People…” link in the top menu and choose “Import from file” option in the menu.

  3. Do not change "Microsoft Outlook (using CSV)" on the Step 2 and choose the file to import on the Step 3: Choose a file above from your hard drive.

  4. Click on the “Import contacts” button and you are done. Now you will not lose any e-mail messages from us.


AOL Users
To make sure that you receive emails from us please follow these instructions:
  1. Download the CSV file (“comma-separated list”) from us to your hard drive: EasyHits4U CSV (right-click "Save as…")

  2. Log into your AOL account, click on the “Contacts” option in the left menu and choose “Tools > Import” option in the top menu.

  3. Choose file above from your hard drive. Do not change default File type: Comma-separated value (CSV).

  4. Click on the “Import” button and you are done. Now you will not lose any e-mail messages from us.


BoxBe Users
Log into your BoxBe account, click on the "Approved Domains" in the right box and add easyhits4u.com OR click on the "Contacts" tab and add news@easyhits4u.com in the "Add new guest email address" form field.

    AutoTrader is South Africa’s leading market place for buying and selling new cars and used cars, bikes, boats, bakkies, SUV’s, commercial vehicles, plant and farm and more. We advertise the widest range of new cars and second-hand cars for sale across South Africa, with hundreds of new and used car makes and models to choose from at any one time. Our interactive website makes buying and selling cars fast, intuitive and reliable.

    Buying your ideal car has never been easier. AutoTrader allows you to search and compare cars to ensure you find the perfect one to meet your needs and fit your budget. You can browse our stock of new and old cars and other vehicles at any time of the day or night, from anywhere and on any screen – mobile, tablet or desktop any way you choose, e.g. by make, model or body type, car price and more using our interactive online search function. As a car buyer, you can narrow down your search by geographical location, or view the contact details of a dealer close to you using our car dealer directory, from anywhere in South Africa.

    If you want to sell your car fast, we’re here for you. Whether you are a private seller or a car dealer, we will get your car classified ad in front of South Africa’s largest automotive audience so you can get your car sold as quickly as possible. Our audience are serious car buyers looking to buy their next dream car or expand their business with a commercial vehicle. Expect a quality response to your advertisement.

    Do all your car research online by reading the latest car news, reviews and watch expert car videos on our website.

At MasterCard, we strive to provide flexible, secure and processing solutions that help customers.

We offer the platforms, expertise and solutions to help customers grow and attract new business through an end-to-end payments solution.


To help drive success, we are organized into three business lines:


  • Payment Transaction Services
  • Prepaid Management Services
  • Payment Gateway Services

Payment Transaction Services

We provide a wide array of issuer and acquirer processing services, including:

  • Full-service credit processing for issuers and large retailers in select regions
  • Full-service debit and prepaid processing for issuers
  • A brand agnostic service for enabling acquirers and their merchants to accept credit, debit and prepaid card payments

As a world-class payments platform and service provider, the Payment Transaction Services is committed to:

  • Operational excellence
  • Solid economics
  • Innovation with focused product development and reinvestment

Acquirer Processing Services

With our Acquirer Processing Services, we offer you end-to-end solutions across the payments industry, including banks, financial institutions, retailers, exchange houses and telecoms. Our suite of products enable customers to electronically process in real-time, card transactions, online transactions, mobile payments & services, and contactless payments. It also helps you meet all your acquiring needs like ATM Driving, POS Driving and Merchant Management.

This is a powerful payment processing service that acquires, authenticates and routes financial transactions from multiple channels, such as Automated Teller Machines (ATM), Point of Sale (POS) terminals, mobiles, Interactive Voice Response (IVR) and kiosks to account management hosts, utility companies, service providers and interchanges or domestic networks.

The robust and tested solution is designed for multi-institution, multi-country, multi-currency environments that supports all acquiring requirements.

Issuer Processing Services

A comprehensive issuer processing service that helps our customers increase revenues by deploying hassle-free products that are secure, flexible and robust. We offer debit, credit and prepaid card issuer processing services to financial institutions to help them serve their customers with only the best.

Our comprehensive service allows you to increase revenue, reduce fraud, streamline your operations and meet regulatory guidelines. These processing services support all major card brands and provide seamless benefits across multiple markets.

Prepaid Management Services

  • Coordination of program operations and acting as point of contact for the program owning organization (e.g.: bank, distributing merchant or airline)
  • Core prepaid program management including program-level design, delivery and ongoing management
  • Customer services for cardholders and customers
  • Card products — Consumer multicurrency and corporate travel prepaid cards; Cash Passport or white-labeled branding

Payment Gateway Services

Our payment gateway service offerings include:

  • A service for merchants, enabling them to accept electronic payments by a variety of methods and channels
  • A service for acquirers, enabling their merchant clients to accept electronic payments by a variety of payment methods
  • Gateway services that feature a platform that provides financial institutions and merchants with a single connection for all transaction switching

Our offerings are based on the MasterCard standard of innovation, safety and security. We are driven by smart technology, data insights and leverage the strength and breadth of MasterCard solutions to meet your acquiring or issuing needs.

China Post Register Airmail

China Post Register Mail is an economical and slow shipping service. Normally it will take about 2 to 4 weeks to arrive destination country from China.

Register Airmail tracking number is a 13-digit tracking code starting with letter "R" and ending with "CN". i.e. RR123456789CN.
Click here to China Post Register Airmail

China Post EMS/ePacket

China EMS/ePacket is a fast global courier service by China Post. Normally it will take about 1 to 2 weeks to arrive destination country from China.

China Post EMS tracking number is a 13-digit tracking code starting with letter "E" and ending with "CN". i.e. EE123456789CN
click to track China Post EMS/ePacket

Hong Kong Post/Singapore Post tracking

Some China shippers use Hong Kong Post and Singapore Post to ship battery item. The parcel will be shipped to Hong Kong or Singapore first, then transit to other countries. Arrival time might be arround 3 to 6 weeks

Hong Kong Post tracking# ending with HK i.e RA123456789HK, Singapore Post tracking number ending with SG, i.e RA123456789SG.

Click the "show map" checkbox to display spots in the google map. The map will always load the spots which you see in the "select spot" menu.

For example: select Italy, the spot menu will fill with all spots in Italy and they will also load into the map and display. If you want to see all spots in Europe then select ALL in country menu. You can even display ALL spots available by selecting ALL continents and then ALL in countries, but remember this will load a lot of spots and will be slow...

Internet Explorer is not recommended browser for the spot map, it is very slow compared to other modern browsers. Firefox, Google Chrome, Opera or Safari are the best options.

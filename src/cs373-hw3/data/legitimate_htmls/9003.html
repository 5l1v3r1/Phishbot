The Campaign For...
SDSU HAS RAISED MORE THAN $630 MILLION TO SUPPORT STUDENTS, FACULTY AND STAFF. THERE IS MORE WORK TO BE DONE, SO WE'VE SET A NEW GOAL OF $750 MILLION!
Gloria

As an associate librarian, Gloria spearheads SDSU’s oral history project documenting the African American presence in San Diego. Library faculty and staff produce original research and support information access for the campus community.

John

Marine Corps veteran John San Miguel interned at SPAWAR through SDSU’s Troops to Engineers program. Currently working on a master’s degree in computer science, John is now a full-time SPAWAR professional and a mentor to SDSU student veterans.

Tenille
Donor-supported research endowments allow students like Tenille to gain valuable hands-on experiences and enrich their learning by working directly with faculty to address challenges in basic science and the social sciences.
Isaac
As a faculty artist in the School of Music and Dance and a founding member of the Hausmann Quartet, Isaac is an example of SDSU’s commitment to the creativity. Philanthropy supports the visual and performing arts and scholarships for aspiring artists.

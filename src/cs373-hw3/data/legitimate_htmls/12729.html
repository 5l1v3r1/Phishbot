Wakanow | Online Travel Booking Site in Nigeria


Wakanow is Africa’s leading full-service, online travel company. At Wakanow, we understand that everyone likes easy and affordable travel services, so we provide our travelers with a one stop portal for Flights, Hotels, Visa assistance, Holiday packages, Airport transfers, an international travel SIM and much more. Our booking portal provide transparency to our customers and gives them the ability to research, plan, and book all their domestic and international travel from the comfort of their homes, offices or anywhere else they are. Besides our online booking portal, we also have a thriving call center as well as physical travel centers across Nigeria. Our travel centers number over 25 and still counting with one center strategically placed at the international airports for customer convenience. We have offices in Ghana, London and Dubai and plan to expand into the USA and other African countries in 2016. Our Partners are our key to operations and ensure that we keep the good travel deals a constant feature in our offering.

What is Identity Theft?

Thieves pretend to be you to take over or open new accounts, file fake tax returns, rent or buy properties, or do other criminal things in your name.

What is Lifelock Identity Theft Protection?

LifeLock helps protect your identity with proprietary technology to detect and alert you to ID threats.† If you ever have an identity theft problem while a LifeLock member, our U.S.-based team of specialists will work to resolve it.

Washington Talent Agency
475 Reviews

We used Black Tie as our wedding band and Da Vinci strings for our ceremony and cocktail music. Very happy with both.

We decided on Black Tie off a recommendation from a co-worker who used them for his wedding. They were fantastic. Strickland, the band leader, was so helpful in our pre-wedding consultation when it came to working through the whole night's timeline and making song recommendations. The rest of the band (two guitar players, a drummer, a saxophone player, a keyboardist and three singers) was amazing and so receptive to my husband and me throughout the night. They played all the genres of music we asked for and even learned a song that they didn't previously know that had special meaning to my family. The music was the number one most important thing for my husband and he was really happy with them.

As for Da Vinci, they were also great. The pre-ceremony and ceremony music was tasteful and just what we were looking for. They also played more upbeat and contemporary music during cocktails, which was nice.

Finally, our agent at WTA, Shana Lang, was really flexible and made sure to get us what wanted. She also followed up with us afterward to make sure we were happy night of. My only complaint is that there were a few times that I had to send her a follow-up email when I didn't hear back from an original email, but I understand that these agents work with a lot of clients at one time. She always responded after my follow-up.

Definitely recommend Washington Talent Agency and Black Tie!!
Da Vinci's Florist & Lighting
417 Reviews

Let me say right off the bat that Da Vinci supplies beautiful flowers. I had some fairly unique requests because I didn't want my flowers to look like every other wedding's flowers. Sharone (the owner) understood my vision right away and delivered exactly what I was looking for. I even suggested something he said he had never done but he had no problem executing what I wanted.

Also helpful is that they are able to supply more than just flowers. We did a few rentals with them (columns for the flowers to sit on during the ceremony, a Chuppah, tea candles, table cloths, etc.) Made things a lot easier than having to go to another company for rentals.

And I found the pricing to be fair for the flowers and the rental items. Only complaint with pricing was that they charged for their own staff to move items from the ceremony to the reception, and they added a standard service charge to THAT service charge.

Now for the negatives. The consultant we were supposed to work with at our first appointment was running behind with another client (like an hour behind) but no one ever bothered to tell us that. So we just sat in the waiting room for an hour with the receptionist saying "she'll be right with you" every 10 minutes. Finally, Sharone came out and worked with us directly after we said we were going to leave. They are also a little bit disorganized behind the scenes, as they only have on person doing proposals and the business side of things and sometimes it required multiple follow-ups on that end. But everything we ordered arrived on the wedding day and they even brought some additional items we didn't pay for (like extra linens), which was nice.

Overall I would recommend them as a one-stop shop if you're looking for a variety of things out of your florist. Just go into the process with some patience and you'll have a positive experience.
Carlyle Club
68 Reviews

Sharon and her team were amazing. Everyone said we had one the best weddings overall but particularly raved about the uniqueness of the reception venue, loved the food, and thought the service was excellent. As many others said; Sharon and her staff get your vision and add special touches throughout the day that I didn't even ask for. They worked with us to create a unique menu for a great price and gave great advice throughout the process. I really appreciated how much was included in the price, what they already had for decor and of course the quality of the food and service. Sharon can be a little hard to get a hold of months before the wedding, but the couple weeks leading up to your big day she is very attentive. Overall I can't recommend the Carlyle Club enough if you really want a unique venue, with great staff, and excellent food.

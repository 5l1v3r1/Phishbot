So, on an uber-conservative radio show appearance, did the usual spiel about how all of America's woes are because Christianity has slightly less absolute hegemonic control than it used to. This is the usual whining that comes from any group that's used to being in power -- they see a push for equality, or just slightly less inequality, and they see it as an attack. Insecure men think that feminism is out to oppress all men, and some far right Christians think that other religions (especially Islam) or the non-religious are going to impose their views upon them.

And they can believe that if they want. In fact, Anne Graham has the right to believe that, theologically, any or all disasters are the result of divine wrath.

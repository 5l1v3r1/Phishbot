GCU Spotlight

Riley Wiedmeier

B.S. in Biology with an Emphasis in Pre-Physician Assistant

"The instructors are incredible. They are one of the main reasons why I love Grand Canyon University. They provide courses that are educational and challenging. Plus, it is very rare to find a school in which professors actually get to know their students. Class sizes are perfect for learning, and it is easy to ask questions."

Sara Torrez

M.S. in Leadership

"My classes are rich in study and the professors are amazing. Each class is unique and provides me with the critical thinking skills that I need for the future. The instructors are fantastic. They are responsive, engaging and supportive. They foster an environment where students can participate and interact freely."

Irma Crawford

MBA and MSN with an Emphasis in Nursing Leadership in Health Care Systems

"After completing my BSN through Grand Canyon University, I decided to continue on the path to nursing leadership and improving nursing care by obtaining a MBA/MSN degree. This required for me to research different universities and their respective programs. Comparatively, GCU came out ahead. It offered many discounts that made a graduate degree affordable, and offered convenient, online coursework. GCU ranked very high in how it compared to other notable MSN/MBA programs. Currently, I am almost halfway through my dual degree and once more GCU is making this nurse's dream a soon-to-be reality."

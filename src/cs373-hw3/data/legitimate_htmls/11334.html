McGill joins GULF

McGill Principal Suzanne Fortier has accepted an invitation to join the Global University Leaders Forum (GULF), an intellectual community made up of 26 of the world’s top universities. McGill is the only Canadian member of GULF, whose role is to advise World Economic Forum leaders on matters relating to higher education and research.

Department and University Information

McGill University

Our 11 faculties and 11 professional schools offer more than 300 programs to some 40,000 graduate, undergraduate and continuing studies students.

McGill ranks 1st in Canada among medical-doctoral universities (Maclean’s) and 24th in the world (QS World University Rankings).

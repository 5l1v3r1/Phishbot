Yo Manga vs. Hey Manga
Over the last couple of weeks, there's been some disputes between the scanlation group Yo Manga and the aggregate/scanlation group heymanga.xyz.

  • YoManga accused HeyManga of using their raws without credit and hosting their releases without permission. And apparently the hosting is automated, as I've seen it happen within minutes of the release going up...
  • HeyManga ignores the accusations and threatens with DDoS
  • YoManga's site goes down due to DDoS (although no one can pin it on HeyManga)
  • When YoManga comes back up, they start hosting HeyManga's releases without permission (and removing HeyManga's credit pages)


So it's basically like fighting fire with fire. Neither side appears to be letting up. Even earlier today, YoManga put up HeyManga's releases of Si-Eun.

In response, we've removed all of both groups' releases from MangaUpdates.

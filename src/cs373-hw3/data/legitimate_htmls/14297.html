              UA President Johnsen announces Dr. Thomas as UAF Interim Chancellor

              Press Release - July 1, 2016

              FAIRBANKS — University of Alaska President Jim Johnsen has appointed Dana L. Thomas as interim chancellor of the University of Alaska Fairbanks. Thomas will begin on Aug. 14, succeeding outgoing UAF Interim Chancellor Mike Powers, and will end his term on June 30, 2017.

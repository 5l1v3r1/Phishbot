Mickey by Toni Basil

"Mickey" by Toni Basil was originally a song called "Kitty" by a male group. She picked the new name after Micky Dolenz of The Monkees.

More facts about "Mickey"

Frank Sinatra

Long before Ol' Blue Eyes became "The Voice" or married Hollywood starlet Ava Gardner, Sinatra was a scrappy troublemaker growing up in Hoboken, New Jersey. As a teen, he learned music by ear and started singing professionally but, despite becoming a legendary crooner, he never learned how to read music.

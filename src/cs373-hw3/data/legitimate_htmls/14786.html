emaze is the next generation of online presentation technology,
enabling beautiful presentation design automatically.
With more than 20M presentations created on the platform, emaze
big-data and machine learning algorithms utilize crowd wisdom
to re-design PowerPoint presentations into impressive storytelling.
State of the art HTML5 technology enables the addition of gifs,
media, live data, hyperlinking and more.

Welcome to ICD10Data.com

ICD10Data.com is a free reference website designed for the fast lookup of the new American ICD-10-CM (diagnosis) and ICD-10-PCS (procedure) medical billing codes.

ICD-10-CM/PCS replaced ICD-9-CM on Thursday October 1, 2015, therefore all claims with a date of service on or after October 1 require the use of ICD-10-CM/PCS codes.

ICD10Data.com is now more smartphone and tablet friendly - please do not hesitate to access this website on your iPad, iPhone or Android device.

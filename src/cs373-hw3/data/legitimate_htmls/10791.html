Best flight deals: airberlin

airberlin is one of the leading airlines in Europe and offers flights to 147 destinations worldwide each year. The second largest airline in Germany carried more than 30.2 million passengers in 2015. airberlin offers a global route network through its strategic partnership with Etihad Airways, which has a 29.21% share in airberlin, and through membership of the oneworld® airline alliance. topbonus, the frequent flyer program of airberlin is one of the leading programs in Europe with more than 4 million members. The airline with the award-winning service operates codeshare flights worldwide with 21 airlines. The fleet is among the most modern and eco-efficient in Europe. Together with other airlines, airberlin belongs to Etihad Airways Partners, a new brand with which Etihad has been uniting shared activities since the end of 2014.
 

 
Flights with airberlin: excellent quality and service guaranteed

airberlin's appealing price/performance ratio throughout its flights is highly attractive to both business and non-business travellers. A choice of gourmet meals from the "Sansibar" Restaurant in Sylt is available on most airberlin flights with a minimum 60-minute flight time. Price reductions for children and a free baggage allowance of at least 23 kilograms in almost all fare categories round off the services offered. On long-haul routes airberlin guests enjoy an individual In-Seat Entertainment program and complimentary alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks are served. With the FullFlat seats it provides, airberlin's Business Class offers a comfortable flight experience which meets the highest expectations. airberlin's topbonus frequent flyer programme is free of charge. The programme allows airberlin customers to earn miles which they can later redeem for attractive premium flights. airberlin flights can be booked at airberlin.com, at travel agents or by calling the airline's Service Center.

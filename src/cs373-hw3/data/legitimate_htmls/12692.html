About Spreaker

Create your own Podcast with Spreaker

Spreaker.com has been giving podcasters simple, yet high-quality tools to create their own podcast since 2010. Anyone can create, host, distribute, and measure their audio content with ease.

Website and mobile apps for iOS and Android offer easy to use tools that guide podcasters through the entire creation process. Features include a recording console, audio storage space for hosting shows, the ability to connect with social networks and embed players, detailed audience analytics, and so much more.

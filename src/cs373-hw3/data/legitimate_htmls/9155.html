 WELCOME

Founded in 1993, Smart Link Corporation is a developer and publisher of multilingual software products and web-based solutions. Since 2000, the company has focused on designing a complete, yet affordable business solution for multilingual communications by developing and integrating innovative language technologies in the Internet and Intranet environments.
Translator Spell Checker
Online Translator performs real-time instant translation of words, phrases and texts between 66 languages in more than 4,200 language combinations. The translation is powered by Google Translate, Microsoft Translator, Babylon Translator and other machine translation engines.

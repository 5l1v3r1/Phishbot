Modern Warfare Remastered

Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare Remastered is Back!

Relive one of the most iconic campaigns of all-time, that includes fan favorite missions "All Ghillied Up," and "Crew Expendable."

One of the most criticallly-acclaimed games in history, Call of Duty: Modern Warfare is back, remastered in stunning high-definition for PS4, Xbox One, and PC! Relive one of the most iconic campaigns of all-time including fan favorite missions "All Ghillied Up," and "Crew Expendable." Also, experience the online multiplayer that redefined Call of Duty! Battle head-to-head in 10 fan-favorite maps from the original multiplayer mode, including classics such as "Crash," "Backlot," and "Crossfire."

*Call of Duty: Modern Warfare Remastered contains only 10 MP maps from the original Call of Duty: Modern Warfare game. For more information, please visit www.callofduty.com/MWR_FAQ.

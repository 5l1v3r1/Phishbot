Research

Courses and programs Research at UWA

Male bees fight back against STDs

02 August 2016

Scientists at The University of Western Australia are a step closer to protecting honey bees from a widespread disease that causes dysentery and weakens hives considerably.

Stable housing for homeless will save millions

02 August 2016

A new study from UWA has found that providing stable public housing for homeless people could save the Western Australian health system more than $16 million a year.

New engineering program aims to deliver medical breakthroughs

28 July 2016

A new engineering program will enable bioengineers to work on technologies to improve health outcomes, such as reducing the amount of surgery needed by cancer patients and developing equipment to detect people at risk of heart attacks.

UWA flaming seed team finalists in WA Innovator of the Year Competition

28 July 2016

Researchers at UWA who invented a new technique for ‘flaming’ wild seeds have been nominated as finalists in the WA Innovator of the Year competition.

Researchers named finalists in Premier's Science Awards

25 July 2016

Eight leading researchers from The University of Western Australia are among 15 finalists announced in this year’s Premier’s Science Awards.

3D-printing used to explore superconductivity of materials

19 July 2016

Researchers at The University of Western Australia have revealed that 3D-printing can be used to create a resonant microwave cavity via an aluminium-silicon alloy that shows superconductivity when cooled below the critical temperature of aluminium.

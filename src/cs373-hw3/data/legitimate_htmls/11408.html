CheetahMail

Email marketing software that scales

 Experian email marketing software, CheetahMail, is a data-driven email technology that enables clients to build relevant relationships and personalized communication with customers.

Delivering personalized marketing experiences

Drive deeper engagement creating 1-to-1 communication streams to deliver superior customer experiences.

Execute precise email marketing campaigns. Deploy and test your targeted emailing lists in real time, based on behavior, to launch efficient and effective marketing campaigns.

Reach the right customer with segmented emails. Optimize email strategies using the advance segmentation technology to reach valuable targeted audiences, anywhere.

Track your email campaigns. Measure and improve your email marketing ROI with CheetahMail reporting capability by tracking email campaign performance and subscriber's engagement.

Build quality customer data. Experian CheetahMail data management system easily collects customer's data in a secure way and facilitates the integration of quality contact data.

Taking your marketing strategy further

The complete Experian Marketing Suite platform allows you to take control of your customer's data, optimizing your engagement strategy to deliver intelligent interactions across all channels, in real time, every time.

Engage your customers across multiple channels. Identify customers across channels to create, maintain and enhance over time deep customer identity profiles to build exceptional experiences.

Leverage accurate behavioral data. Develop your customer's data around actual consumer behavior and preferences to deliver personalized intelligent content and create meaningful client relations.

Increase your revenue with real time contextual data. Create highly customized client experiences using contextual data points such as time, location and device to connect in real time.

Bringing unique email marketing results

For over a decade, global brand leaders have chosen Experian CheetahMail to execute their marketing strategy. Sending more than 10 billion emails a month and backed by the industry's highest rated client services team, CheetahMail's advance email technology has helped over 2,000 brands maximize the relevance of their communication and drastically increase revenue and customer loyalty.

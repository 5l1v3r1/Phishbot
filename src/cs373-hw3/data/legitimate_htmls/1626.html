Provide Orphans with 480 Gallons of Clean Running Water: Completed May 9, 2016

96,000 daily clicks funded this project – thank you!

By installing a unique solar-powered well system, children at the Dream Home will have access to a clean, renewable water source for years to come. Children will be able to receive water directly from a faucet. This piped water supply will enhance cleanliness, providing better health and sanitation for the children living at this orphanage.

This campaign is complete.

Send 4 Cooling Vests To Deployed Military Dogs To Prevent Heat Exahustion: Completed May 6, 2016

60,000 daily clicks funded this project – thank you!

GreaterGood.org’s Pets and Vets program is granting support to partners that are sending care packages to military war dogs throughout the world. Specialty equipment is requested by handlers to our partners who are able to provide items needed to protect, train and care for their military war dogs. These supplies are crucial to the health and well being of the dogs and their handlers. Please help send supplies now!

Provide for the Partial Cost of 12 Mammograms: Completed May 4, 2016

60,000 daily clicks funded this project – thank you!

Breast cancer is no longer a disease found primarily in wealthier countries; worldwide, this scourge is on the rise. But screening is not as readily available in some parts of the world. In Sierra Leone, there is only one mammography machine, and getting a mammogram can be prohibitively expensive. You can help mothers, sisters, daughters, and friends in Sierra Leone.

This campaign is complete.

Provide Four Students in Rural Africa with Bicycles: Completed May 2, 2016

117,600 daily clicks funded this project – thank you!

Many individuals in rural Africa do not have a means of transportation. A lack of adequate mobility causes a plethora of issues for locals— from impeding education to delaying timely access to medical attention. You can help. Your donation will go towards providing a high-quality bicycle to a student in rural Africa.

Save 40 Acres of Tropical Rainforest & Tiger Habitat: Completed April 29, 2016

80,000 daily clicks funded this project – thank you!

The vast majority of Sumatra’s spectacular rainforests have been rampantly destroyed in recent decades to make way for growing swaths of oil palm and rubber plantations. That’s why Rainforest Trust is working with local partner FKL to strategically purchase private properties in the region to establish the Kluet Wildlife Reserve. This reserve will prevent further colonization, deforestation, and protect against wildlife poaching in the region. You can help. Just $9.82 saves an acre of tropical rainforest in the Leuser Ecosystem.

This campaign is complete.
Read more here!

Help Prevent Polar Bear Extinction Through Conservation and Vital Research: Completed April 27, 2016

80,000 daily clicks funded this project – thank you!

With increased knowledge, research can assist in identifying habitats to protect, as the polar bear is completely reliant on this specialized living environment. By increasing the areas that are protected, polar bears will have larger spaces for denning and raising their cubs. You can help. Your donation will go toward research and the conservation of diminishing polar bear habitat. It is imperative to protect polar bear habitat now before it’s too late.

This campaign is complete.
Read more here!

Feed 5 Orphaned Chimpanzee Babies For 3 Months: Completed April 25, 2016

120,000 daily clicks funded this project – thank you!

Chimpanzees and gorillas in protected forests in Africa are shot down and butchered in the bushmeat trade, and the babies, who are too small to eat, are sold on the black market to be pets. In other areas, baby primates are stolen from the forest to be trafficked to China or the Middle East where they are sold as pets or put on exhibit in appallingly inhumane zoos. Thousands of chimpanzees and other primates who have been rescued as babies from the horrific wildlife trade now live at Pan African Sanctuary Alliance (PASA) member wildlife centers across Africa. You can help. Just $10 feeds a baby chimpanzee for one week.

This campaign is complete.

Real Impact: Act Where It Matters

With so many simple, easy ways to be involved – from a free click to a direct donation that funds a specific program – your smallest actions online matter, creating real results at GreaterGood.

Already, your actions have given $30 million to nonprofit charities around the world since 1999. That means millions have received help and assistance because of your efforts: hungry families fed, pets rescued, rainforests saved, breast cancer caught and treated, children helped, and so much more.

What You Can Do: Small Actions, Big Results

We invite you to explore the network of great charities and good works that make up GreaterGood. Find the cause, program, or project that speaks to your passion for helping others and start making a difference today.

We believe that no good action is too small and, with all the new tools at our websites, together the results are greater than ever before. Make a daily click, sign a petition, donate what you can, shop where it matters, and help us spread the word. You can also visit us at our new store in the Mall of America, located in Bloomington, Minnesota. Just like our online shops, every purchase gives back to a worthy cause!

Every day our community grows larger and the good that we have done together grows greater. Every day we’re adding new pieces to our story. Subscribe to our newsletters, read our blogs, or join us on Facebook to learn even more about how your actions continue to make a difference daily.

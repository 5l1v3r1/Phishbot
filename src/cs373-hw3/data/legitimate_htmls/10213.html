For more than a decade, immihelp®.com is a premier resource of comprehensive information about U.S. legal immigration. It provides detailed information for visitors visa, student visa and all other types of visas such as business, fiance, H, L, and many others. It also provides information about all types of green card (employment or family based or other), including various processes at various steps, in the U.S. and abroad. It is also a great resource for visitors medical insurance, Schengen visa, PIO/OCI as well information for travel to the U.S. and newcomer to the U.S.

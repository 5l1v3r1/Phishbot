Illuminated Cosmetic Update

Sigils LT posted Fri at 3:38

Oh hellooooo everyone, fancy meeting you here! I'm really excited about this week's update. We've been working on this one for several months and it's got a ton of pretty stuff. This update releases over 55 new cosmetic items including: Minestrike Weapon Skins, new Particles, Double Jump Effects, Death Effects, Arrow Trails, and a new type of cosmetic Win Effects!

Minestrike Weapon skins are finally here! There are 18 skins for you to collect and equip to your weapons. They are also all StatTracker skins which will keep track of your kills with that particular weapon.

Win Effects introduce a new ending to all Mineplex games. Now you will see a special animation that plays at the end of the game highlighting the winner or the MVP of the winning team. Keep your eyes out for the Baby Chicken one, that's my favorite.

We're also excited to reveal our newest chest the Illuminated Chest. We've heard feedback from the community that some players don't like the current chest system that gives duplicates, so we're offering something new. The Illuminated Chest will only allow players to open a single chest, but every chest will give a new cosmetic item. If you have every cosmetic item unlocked, you won't be able to open the chest until we add new items in the future. We're really excited to hear your feedback on this new system as we try to find one that works better for everyone.

Finally, I just wanted to take a second to address a lot of comments we've been getting lately about various updates and systems that you'd like us to implement (like AntiCheat). Just know that we are listening and we do look at all of your feedback. We're working on some solutions and upgrades but they take time. Just because we finish an update that we've been working on for several months with one developer doesn't mean we aren't working on other things too. With that in mind leave your feedback on the forums.

MINEPLEX + MAVERICKS

Sigils LT posted Wed at 17:40

Mineplex has partnered with the Dallas Mavericks for an AWESOME project. Soon sports fans around the world will be able to check out a life size re-creation of the American Airlines Stadium in Minecraft, hosted right here on Mineplex. All content will be free for everyone. Anyone can come tour the stadium and try out the activities. 

More details can be expected as we get closer to launch early this summer. Check out the interview with Mavericks owner Mark Cuban for more info.

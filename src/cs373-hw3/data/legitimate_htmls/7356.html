How SiteJabber is different

SiteJabber is the only review platform that does not charge businesses to manage their reputation. Businesses can respond to reviews and collect reviews from customers for free, but they cannot pay to remove bad reviews. We believe this is a fairer system for businesses and consumers, as it's much easier to see which businesses have legitimately earned good and bad reviews instead of merely showing which businesses are willing to pay the most to manage their reputations.
